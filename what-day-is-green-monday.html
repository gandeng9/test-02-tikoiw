Introduction

Green Monday is a term that has been coined by retailers to define a shopping day that falls on the second Monday of December. It is similar to Black Friday and Cyber Monday, and it is considered to be one of the biggest online shopping days of the year. In this article, we will discuss what Green Monday is and why it is important for retailers and shoppers alike.

What is Green Monday?

Green Monday is a term that was first used by eBay in 2007 to describe the second Monday of December, which is usually the last day to order items online and receive them before Christmas. It is called Green Monday because it is a day when retailers see a significant increase in sales, and they make a lot of "green" (money).

Why is Green Monday Important?

Green Monday is important for both retailers and shoppers. For retailers, it is a day when they can make a lot of sales and increase their revenue. For shoppers, it is a day when they can find great deals and discounts on items that they want to buy for Christmas.

When is Green Monday in 2023?

In 2023, Green Monday will fall on December 11th. This is the second Monday of December, and it is the day when retailers will offer great deals and discounts to shoppers.

How to Prepare for Green Monday

If you want to take advantage of the deals and discounts on Green Monday, you need to be prepared. Here are some tips to help you prepare for Green Monday:

Make a List

Make a list of the items that you want to buy on Green Monday. This will help you stay focused and avoid getting distracted by other deals that you don't really need.

Compare Prices

Before you buy anything on Green Monday, make sure that you compare prices. Check different websites and retailers to see who is offering the best deal.

Use Coupons and Promo Codes

Many retailers offer coupons and promo codes on Green Monday. Make sure that you take advantage of these discounts to save even more money.

Conclusion

Green Monday is an important day for both retailers and shoppers. It is a day when retailers make a lot of sales, and shoppers can find great deals and discounts on items that they want to buy for Christmas. If you want to take advantage of the deals and discounts on Green Monday, make sure that you are prepared and follow the tips that we have provided in this article. Happy shopping!
