Jobs Near Me Over $16 An Hour: Your Ultimate Guide

Introduction

Are you tired of working minimum wage jobs that barely pay your bills? Are you looking for a job that pays over $16 an hour? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll discuss some of the best jobs near you that pay over $16 an hour.

Why $16 an Hour?

$16 an hour is a decent wage that can help you cover your basic needs and maybe even save some money. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median hourly wage for all occupations in the United States is $19.14. So, finding a job that pays over $16 an hour is definitely possible.

Jobs That Pay Over $16 an Hour

Retail Jobs

Retail jobs are great for people who love interacting with customers and have good communication skills. Some examples of retail jobs that pay over $16 an hour include sales associates, customer service representatives, and merchandisers.

Healthcare Jobs

If you're interested in the healthcare industry, there are plenty of jobs that pay over $16 an hour. Some examples of healthcare jobs include medical assistants, dental assistants, and phlebotomists.

Warehouse Jobs

Warehouse jobs are physically demanding, but they pay well. Some examples of warehouse jobs that pay over $16 an hour include forklift operators, pickers/packers, and material handlers.

Food Service Jobs

If you're passionate about food, there are plenty of food service jobs that pay over $16 an hour. Some examples include line cooks, sous chefs, and bakers.

Transportation Jobs

If you have a driver's license and a clean driving record, there are plenty of transportation jobs that pay over $16 an hour. Some examples include delivery drivers, bus drivers, and truck drivers.

How to Find Jobs Near You

Online Job Boards

Online job boards like Indeed and Monster are great resources for finding jobs near you. You can create a profile, upload your resume, and apply to jobs with just a few clicks.

Networking

Networking is also a great way to find jobs near you. Talk to your friends, family members, and acquaintances to see if they know of any job openings in your area.

Local Newspapers

Local newspapers often have job listings in the classifieds section. Pick up a copy of your local newspaper and check the classifieds for job openings.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are plenty of jobs near you that pay over $16 an hour. Whether you're interested in healthcare, retail, or transportation, there's a job out there for you. Use online job boards, networking, and local newspapers to find job openings in your area. Good luck!
