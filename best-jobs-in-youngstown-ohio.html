Best Jobs In Youngstown Ohio In 2023

Introduction

Youngstown, Ohio is a city that has been making a comeback in recent years. With new businesses moving in and a growing economy, there are many job opportunities available. In this article, we will explore some of the best jobs in Youngstown, Ohio in 2023.

Healthcare Jobs

One of the most in-demand fields in Youngstown, Ohio is healthcare. From nurses to doctors to medical assistants, there are a variety of positions available. The average salary for healthcare jobs in Youngstown is around $60,000 per year.

Technology Jobs

Another growing industry in Youngstown is technology. With companies like Turning Technologies and VEC, there are many job opportunities for software developers, IT professionals, and engineers. The average salary for technology jobs in Youngstown is around $80,000 per year.

Manufacturing Jobs

Youngstown has a rich history in manufacturing, and that industry is still alive and well today. With companies like General Motors and Vallourec, there are many job opportunities for skilled tradespeople and engineers. The average salary for manufacturing jobs in Youngstown is around $50,000 per year.

Education Jobs

Youngstown has a number of colleges and universities, including Youngstown State University and Eastern Gateway Community College. With these institutions come job opportunities in teaching, administration, and student services. The average salary for education jobs in Youngstown is around $45,000 per year.

Finance Jobs

As the economy in Youngstown continues to grow, so does the need for financial professionals. From accountants to financial analysts to loan officers, there are many job opportunities in this field. The average salary for finance jobs in Youngstown is around $65,000 per year.

Retail Jobs

Youngstown has a number of shopping centers and malls, including the Southern Park Mall and Eastwood Mall. With these shopping centers come job opportunities in sales, customer service, and management. The average salary for retail jobs in Youngstown is around $25,000 per year.

Transportation Jobs

With its location in the Rust Belt, Youngstown has a number of job opportunities in transportation. From truck drivers to logistics professionals to warehouse workers, there are many positions available. The average salary for transportation jobs in Youngstown is around $35,000 per year.

Construction Jobs

As Youngstown continues to grow and expand, so does the need for construction professionals. From carpenters to electricians to plumbers, there are many job opportunities in this field. The average salary for construction jobs in Youngstown is around $40,000 per year.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many job opportunities available in Youngstown, Ohio in 2023. From healthcare to technology to manufacturing, there is something for everyone. With a growing economy and a revitalized city, Youngstown is a great place to live and work.
