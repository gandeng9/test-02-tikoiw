Discover The Best Places To Live In Kansas City In 2023

Introduction

Are you planning to relocate to Kansas City this year? Known for its friendly people, vibrant culture, and booming economy, Kansas City offers a range of neighborhoods to suit different lifestyles and budgets. Whether you're looking for a trendy urban community or a quiet suburban enclave, you'll find plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll explore the top places to live in Kansas City in 2023.

Downtown Kansas City

If you love the energy and excitement of city living, downtown Kansas City is the place for you. With its stunning skyline, world-class restaurants, and cultural attractions, downtown offers a unique blend of history and modernity. You'll find a mix of historic buildings and new developments, including luxury apartments, lofts, and condos. Many residents enjoy walking or biking to work, and there's always something to do, from catching a concert at the Sprint Center to exploring the art galleries in the Crossroads Arts District.

Brookside

Located just south of the Country Club Plaza, Brookside is a charming neighborhood that offers a small-town feel in the heart of the city. With its tree-lined streets, beautiful homes, and local shops and restaurants, Brookside is a popular choice for families and young professionals. You'll find a mix of bungalows, Tudors, and colonials, and many homes have been updated with modern amenities. The Brookside Farmers' Market is a must-visit for fresh produce and artisanal goods.

West Plaza

If you're looking for a neighborhood that's close to everything, West Plaza is a great choice. Located just west of the Country Club Plaza, West Plaza offers easy access to shopping, dining, and entertainment. You'll find a mix of apartments, condos, and single-family homes, many of which feature charming details like stained glass windows and original hardwood floors. The neighborhood is also home to several parks, including Jacob L. Loose Park, which features a rose garden, a lake, and a playground.

Parkville

If you prefer a more rural setting, Parkville is a beautiful community located just north of the city. With its rolling hills, scenic views, and historic downtown, Parkville offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city. You'll find a mix of housing options, from grand estates to cozy cottages, and the community is known for its excellent schools and family-friendly events. The Parkville Farmers' Market is a great place to stock up on locally grown produce and handmade crafts.

Lee's Summit

Located about 20 miles southeast of downtown Kansas City, Lee's Summit is a thriving suburb that offers a high quality of life. With its award-winning schools, beautiful parks, and excellent shopping and dining options, Lee's Summit is a popular choice for families. You'll find a mix of housing options, from affordable starter homes to upscale estates, and the community is known for its friendly atmosphere and community events like the Downtown Days Festival and the Oktoberfest celebration.

Conclusion

Whether you're a young professional, a growing family, or a retiree, Kansas City offers a range of neighborhoods to suit your needs. From the energy and excitement of downtown to the peaceful retreat of Parkville, there's something for everyone in this vibrant and welcoming city. So why wait? Start exploring the best places to live in Kansas City in 2023 today!
