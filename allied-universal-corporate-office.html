Introduction

Allied Universal is one of the largest security and facility services companies in North America. The company provides integrated security services to a wide range of industries, including healthcare, retail, education, and government. With its headquarters located in Santa Ana, California, Allied Universal Corporate Office oversees the operations of more than 200 offices across the United States and Canada.

History

Allied Universal was established in 2016 as a result of a merger between AlliedBarton Security Services and Universal Services of America. The merger created the largest security services company in North America, with more than 140,000 employees. In 2018, Allied Universal acquired U.S. Security Associates, further expanding its operations across the country.

Allied Universal provides a wide range of security and facility services, including manned guarding, remote monitoring, access control, and emergency response. Allied Universal Corporate Office oversees the development and implementation of these services across all of its offices.
