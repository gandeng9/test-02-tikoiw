Introduction

Are you looking for a rewarding career in the healthcare industry? If you're based in Newcastle, New South Wales, you're in luck! NSW Health Pathology is currently hiring for various positions, ranging from medical laboratory scientists to phlebotomists.

What is NSW Health Pathology?

NSW Health Pathology is a state-owned corporation that provides pathology services to the public healthcare system in New South Wales. It has over 60 laboratories across the state, including one in Newcastle.

Why Work for NSW Health Pathology in Newcastle?

Newcastle is a vibrant city that offers a great work-life balance. It has a thriving healthcare industry, with NSW Health Pathology being one of the biggest employers in the sector. Working for NSW Health Pathology in Newcastle can provide you with job security, competitive salaries, and opportunities for career progression.

Available Positions

Medical Laboratory Scientists

Medical laboratory scientists are responsible for performing laboratory tests on patient samples to diagnose diseases. To apply for this position, you will need a degree in medical laboratory science or a related field.

Phlebotomists

Phlebotomists are responsible for collecting blood samples from patients. To apply for this position, you will need a certificate III in pathology collection or a related field.

Pathology Collectors

Pathology collectors are responsible for collecting patient samples, including blood, urine, and tissue samples. To apply for this position, you will need a certificate III in pathology collection or a related field.

Application Process

Conclusion

If you're looking for a rewarding career in the healthcare industry, consider applying for a position at NSW Health Pathology in Newcastle. With a range of available positions, competitive salaries, and opportunities for career progression, NSW Health Pathology is a great place to work.
