Now Hiring In Portland, Oregon: A Guide To Finding Your Dream Job

Introduction

If you're looking for a job in Portland, Oregon, you're in luck. According to recent statistics, the city's unemployment rate is at an all-time low, and there are plenty of opportunities available for job seekers. Whether you're a recent graduate, a seasoned professional, or someone looking for a career change, Portland has something for everyone. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the top industries and companies that are currently hiring in the city.

The Tech Industry

One of the fastest-growing industries in Portland is tech. The city is home to many startups and established companies in the tech sector, such as Intel, HP, and Google. These companies are always on the lookout for talented individuals who can help them innovate and stay ahead of the competition. If you have skills in software engineering, data science, or cybersecurity, you'll find plenty of job opportunities in Portland's tech industry.

Top Tech Companies Hiring in Portland

- Intel - HP - Google - Nike - Adidas - Columbia Sportswear - Tektronix - Cambia Health Solutions - Puppet - Elemental Technologies

The Healthcare Industry

Another industry that's booming in Portland is healthcare. The city is home to some of the best hospitals and clinics in the country, such as Oregon Health & Science University (OHSU) and Legacy Health. These institutions are always looking for skilled healthcare professionals who can provide quality care to patients. If you have a degree in nursing, medicine, or allied health, you'll find many job opportunities in Portland's healthcare industry.

Top Healthcare Companies Hiring in Portland

- Oregon Health & Science University - Legacy Health - Kaiser Permanente - Providence Health & Services - Adventist Health - ZoomCare - CareOregon - PacificSource Health Plans - Moda Health - Health Share of Oregon

The Creative Industry

Portland is also known for its thriving creative industry. The city is home to many artists, designers, and writers who work in fields such as advertising, publishing, and film. If you have a passion for creativity and a skill in writing, design, or photography, you'll find plenty of job opportunities in Portland's creative industry.

Top Creative Companies Hiring in Portland

- Wieden+Kennedy - Instrument - Ziba Design - Swift - CMD - Cinco Design - Sockeye - Coates Kokes - Roundhouse - North

The Hospitality Industry

Portland is also a popular tourist destination, and the city's hospitality industry is thriving as a result. Hotels, restaurants, and bars are always looking for talented individuals who can provide exceptional service to guests. If you have experience in hospitality or a passion for customer service, you'll find many job opportunities in Portland's hospitality industry.

Top Hospitality Companies Hiring in Portland

- McMenamins - Provenance Hotels - The Nines - The Heathman Hotel - The Benson - Salt & Straw - Pok Pok - Le Pigeon - Blue Star Donuts - Stumptown Coffee Roasters

Conclusion

In conclusion, if you're looking for a job in Portland, Oregon, you'll find plenty of opportunities in a variety of industries. Whether you're interested in tech, healthcare, creativity, or hospitality, there are companies in Portland looking for talented individuals like you. With a little research and persistence, you'll be sure to find your dream job in this vibrant and growing city.
