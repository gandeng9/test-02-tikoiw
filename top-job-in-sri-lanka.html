Top Jobs In Sri Lanka In 2023

Introduction

Sri Lanka is a country that has a diverse range of job opportunities that are available for job seekers. With the growing economy, there are more job opportunities available in different sectors, including finance, IT, tourism, and healthcare. In this article, we will discuss the top jobs in Sri Lanka in 2023.

1. Software Engineer

Software engineering is a growing field in Sri Lanka, with companies like Virtusa, IFS, and WSO2 expanding their operations in the country. The demand for software engineers is expected to increase in the coming years, with more companies looking to digitize their operations. The average salary of a software engineer in Sri Lanka is around LKR 1.5 million per annum.

2. Accountant

Finance is a crucial aspect of any business, and accountants play a vital role in managing a company's financial operations. With the growing economy, there is an increasing demand for accountants in Sri Lanka. The average salary of an accountant in Sri Lanka is around LKR 800,000 per annum.

3. Doctor

The healthcare sector in Sri Lanka is rapidly growing, with more hospitals and clinics being built across the country. Doctors are in high demand, with the average salary of a doctor in Sri Lanka being around LKR 2 million per annum.

4. Marketing Manager

Marketing is an essential aspect of any business, and companies are always looking for skilled marketing managers to promote their products and services. The average salary of a marketing manager in Sri Lanka is around LKR 1.2 million per annum.

5. Civil Engineer

Sri Lanka is a country that is undergoing significant infrastructure development, with new highways, bridges, and buildings being constructed. Civil engineers play a vital role in the construction industry, and their demand is expected to increase in the coming years. The average salary of a civil engineer in Sri Lanka is around LKR 1.8 million per annum.

6. Tour Guide

Tourism is a significant contributor to Sri Lanka's economy, with millions of tourists visiting the country every year. Tour guides are in high demand, with the average salary of a tour guide in Sri Lanka being around LKR 500,000 per annum.

7. Human Resource Manager

Human resource management is a crucial aspect of any organization, and companies are always looking for skilled HR managers to manage their employees. The average salary of an HR manager in Sri Lanka is around LKR 1 million per annum.

8. Web Developer

The demand for web developers is expected to increase in the coming years, with more companies looking to establish an online presence. The average salary of a web developer in Sri Lanka is around LKR 1.2 million per annum.

9. Data Analyst

Data analysis is a growing field in Sri Lanka, with companies looking to make data-driven decisions. The average salary of a data analyst in Sri Lanka is around LKR 900,000 per annum.

10. Chef

The hospitality industry is a significant contributor to Sri Lanka's economy, with thousands of tourists visiting the country's restaurants and hotels. Chefs are in high demand, with the average salary of a chef in Sri Lanka being around LKR 600,000 per annum.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Sri Lanka offers a diverse range of job opportunities for job seekers in different sectors. The demand for skilled professionals is expected to increase in the coming years, with the growing economy and infrastructure development. If you are looking for a job in Sri Lanka, the above list can be a good starting point to explore your options.
