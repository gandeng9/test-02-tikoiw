Epcor Edmonton offers a wide range of job opportunities across various departments, including engineering, finance, customer service, operations, and human resources. Some of the jobs available at Epcor Edmonton include:

1. Electrical Engineer

As an electrical engineer at Epcor Edmonton, you will be responsible for designing and maintaining the electrical systems that power the company's operations. You will work closely with other engineers and technicians to ensure that the systems are efficient, safe, and reliable.

2. Customer Service Representative

3. Operations Manager

As an operations manager at Epcor Edmonton, you will oversee the day-to-day operations of the company's facilities, including water treatment plants, power stations, and natural gas pipelines.
