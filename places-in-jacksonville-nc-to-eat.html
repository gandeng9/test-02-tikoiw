Introduction

Jacksonville, NC is a charming city that is home to many delicious restaurants. Whether you are a local or a visitor, you are sure to find a restaurant that suits your taste buds. In this article, we will explore the best places to eat in Jacksonville, NC in 2023. From classic American eats to international cuisine, this city has something for everyone.

The Biscuit & The Bean

The Biscuit & The Bean is a local favorite that serves breakfast and lunch. They are known for their biscuits, which are freshly made and come in a variety of flavors. They also offer a wide range of coffee drinks that will satisfy any caffeine lover. For lunch, they have a selection of sandwiches, salads, and soups.

The Kettle Diner

The Kettle Diner is a classic American diner that serves breakfast all day. They are known for their pancakes, which are fluffy and delicious. They also have a selection of omelets, waffles, and breakfast sandwiches. For lunch, they have burgers, sandwiches, and salads.

Angry Ginger Irish Pub

The Angry Ginger Irish Pub is a unique restaurant that serves Irish cuisine. They are known for their fish and chips, which are crispy and flavorful. They also have a selection of Irish beers and whiskeys. For those who want to try something new, they offer a variety of Irish dishes such as shepherd's pie and bangers and mash.

Thai Peppers

Thai Peppers is a restaurant that serves authentic Thai cuisine. They are known for their Pad Thai, which is made with fresh ingredients and is full of flavor. They also have a selection of curries, stir-fries, and noodle dishes. For those who want to try something new, they offer a variety of vegetarian options.

La Esperanza Mexican Restaurant

La Esperanza Mexican Restaurant is a family-owned restaurant that serves authentic Mexican cuisine. They are known for their tacos, which are made with fresh ingredients and come in a variety of flavors. They also have a selection of burritos, enchiladas, and fajitas. For those who want to try something new, they offer a variety of seafood dishes.

Sumo Bowl

Sumo Bowl is a restaurant that serves Japanese cuisine. They are known for their sushi rolls, which are made with fresh ingredients and are full of flavor. They also have a selection of rice bowls, ramen, and udon noodles. For those who want to try something new, they offer a variety of Japanese dishes such as tempura and teriyaki.

The Blackened Kraken

The Blackened Kraken is a seafood restaurant that serves fresh seafood. They are known for their blackened fish, which is cooked to perfection. They also have a selection of seafood boils, sandwiches, and salads. For those who want to try something new, they offer a variety of seafood dishes such as shrimp and grits and lobster mac and cheese.

Brewed Downtown

Brewed Downtown is a coffee shop that also serves breakfast and lunch. They are known for their coffee drinks, which are made with freshly roasted coffee beans. They also have a selection of breakfast sandwiches, pastries, and salads. For lunch, they have a selection of sandwiches, wraps, and soups.

Swansboro Food & Beverage Co.

Swansboro Food & Beverage Co. is a restaurant that serves American cuisine. They are known for their burgers, which are juicy and delicious. They also have a selection of sandwiches, salads, and seafood dishes. For those who want to try something new, they offer a variety of international dishes such as Korean BBQ tacos and Cuban sandwiches.

Conclusion

Jacksonville, NC has a wide range of restaurants that offer delicious food. From classic American eats to international cuisine, this city has something for everyone. Whether you are a local or a visitor, be sure to check out these top 10 places to eat in Jacksonville, NC in 2023.
