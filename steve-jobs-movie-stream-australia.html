Introduction

The Steve Jobs movie, directed by Danny Boyle, is a biographical drama that depicts the life of Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Inc. The movie is based on the book “Steve Jobs” by Walter Isaacson and stars Michael Fassbender as Steve Jobs. It was released in 2015 and received critical acclaim for its exceptional direction, acting, and screenplay. In this article, we will discuss the Steve Jobs movie stream in Australia and provide a review of the movie.

Plot Summary

The movie revolves around three key events in Steve Jobs’ life, namely the launch of the Macintosh computer in 1984, the launch of the NeXT computer in 1988, and the launch of the iMac in 1998. Each event is depicted in real-time, giving the audience a glimpse into the intense and often chaotic world of product launches. The movie also delves into Jobs’ personal life, including his relationship with his daughter Lisa and his strained relationship with his co-founder, Steve Wozniak.

Acting

The acting in the Steve Jobs movie is outstanding, with Michael Fassbender delivering an exceptional performance as Steve Jobs. He captures Jobs’ mannerisms and speech patterns perfectly, making it easy for the audience to forget that they are watching an actor and not the real Steve Jobs. Kate Winslet also delivers a stand-out performance as Joanna Hoffman, Jobs’ confidante and marketing executive.

Direction and Screenplay

The direction by Danny Boyle is exceptional, with the movie being shot in a stylized and unconventional manner. The real-time depiction of each event adds to the tension and drama of the movie, making it a thrilling watch. The screenplay by Aaron Sorkin is also exceptional, with witty and fast-paced dialogue that keeps the audience engaged throughout the movie.

Soundtrack

The soundtrack of the Steve Jobs movie, composed by Daniel Pemberton, is outstanding. The music adds to the tension and drama of the movie, with each event having its own distinct theme. The soundtrack also includes songs from various artists, including Bob Dylan and The Rolling Stones.

Reception

The Steve Jobs movie received critical acclaim upon its release, with many praising the exceptional direction, acting, and screenplay. The movie was also nominated for various awards, including two Academy Awards. However, the movie did not perform well at the box office, with many attributing this to the unconventional style and subject matter of the movie.

Streaming in Australia

The Steve Jobs movie is available for streaming in Australia on various platforms, including Netflix and Apple TV+. The movie is also available for rent or purchase on platforms such as Google Play and iTunes.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Steve Jobs movie is a must-watch for anyone interested in the life of Steve Jobs and the history of Apple Inc. The exceptional direction, acting, screenplay, and soundtrack make it a thrilling and insightful watch. With the movie available for streaming in Australia, there is no reason not to watch it.
