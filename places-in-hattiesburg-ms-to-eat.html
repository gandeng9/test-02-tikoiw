Introduction

Hattiesburg, MS is a beautiful city in the southern region of the United States. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and mouth-watering cuisine. If you're looking for some delicious places to eat in Hattiesburg, MS, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be exploring some of the best places to eat in the city.

1. Keg & Barrel

If you're looking for a casual and laid-back atmosphere, then Keg & Barrel is the perfect place for you. It is known for its extensive beer selection, as well as its delicious burgers and sandwiches. It's a great spot to grab a drink and catch up with friends.

2. Patio 44

Patio 44 is a seafood restaurant that offers a unique dining experience. It has a beautiful outdoor patio that is perfect for enjoying a meal with friends and family. Their menu features a variety of seafood dishes, including crab cakes, shrimp, and lobster.

3. T-Bones Records & Cafe

If you're a music lover, then T-Bones Records & Cafe is a must-visit spot. It is a record store and cafe that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of delicious sandwiches, salads, and soups. They also have an extensive selection of vinyl records for you to browse through while you enjoy your meal.

4. Crescent City Grill

Crescent City Grill is a New Orleans-style restaurant that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of Cajun and Creole dishes, including gumbo, jambalaya, and po' boys. It's a great spot to grab a drink and enjoy some delicious southern cuisine.

5. Glory Bound Gyro Co.

If you're in the mood for Mediterranean cuisine, then Glory Bound Gyro Co. is the perfect spot for you. Their menu features a variety of delicious gyros, falafel, and hummus. It's a great spot to grab a quick and healthy meal.

6. South Mouth Deli

South Mouth Deli is a deli that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of delicious sandwiches, salads, and soups. It's a great spot to grab a quick and delicious meal.

7. The Depot Kitchen & Market

The Depot Kitchen & Market is a restaurant and market that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of delicious sandwiches, salads, and soups. They also have a market where you can purchase fresh produce and other local products.

8. Cotton Blues

Cotton Blues is a restaurant that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of delicious southern dishes, including fried chicken, mac and cheese, and collard greens. It's a great spot to enjoy some comfort food with friends and family.

9. Sully's Tavern

Sully's Tavern is a sports bar that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of delicious pub food, including burgers, wings, and nachos. It's a great spot to catch a game and enjoy some delicious food.

10. Tabella

Tabella is a restaurant that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features a variety of delicious Italian dishes, including pasta, pizza, and seafood. It's a great spot to enjoy a romantic dinner with your significant other.

Conclusion

Hattiesburg, MS is a city that is known for its delicious cuisine. Whether you're in the mood for seafood, southern comfort food, or international cuisine, there's a restaurant in Hattiesburg that will satisfy your cravings.
