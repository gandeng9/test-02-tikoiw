Latest Agriculture Jobs In Zambia 2023

Introduction

Zambia is a country that heavily relies on agriculture. It is the main source of income for most people living in rural areas. The country has made significant progress in the agricultural sector in recent years, leading to the creation of new job opportunities. In this article, we will explore the latest agriculture jobs in Zambia in 2023.

Agriculture Extension Officers

One of the latest agriculture jobs in Zambia is that of an agriculture extension officer. Their primary role is to provide technical support and advice to farmers on how to improve their crop yields. They work closely with farmers to identify problems and implement solutions. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in agriculture or a related field.

Agronomists

Agronomists are professionals who work in the agricultural sector. They research and develop new farming techniques and technologies to improve crop yields. They work closely with farmers to help them implement these new techniques. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in agronomy or a related field.

Farm Managers

Farm managers are responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of a farm. They ensure that crops are planted, harvested, and sold at the right time. They also manage the farm's finances, including budgeting and record-keeping. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in agriculture or a related field and several years of experience in farm management.

Livestock Production Managers

Livestock production managers are responsible for overseeing the production of livestock, including cows, pigs, and chickens. They ensure that the animals are healthy and that they are producing enough milk, eggs, or meat. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in animal science or a related field.

Soil Scientists

Soil scientists study the physical, chemical, and biological properties of soil. They analyze soil samples to determine the best crops to grow in a particular area. They also develop methods to improve soil fertility and prevent soil erosion. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in soil science or a related field.

Agricultural Engineers

Agricultural engineers design and develop new farming equipment and machinery. They also improve existing equipment to make it more efficient and cost-effective. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in agricultural engineering or a related field.

Sales Representatives

Sales representatives work for companies that sell agricultural products, such as seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides. They are responsible for promoting these products to farmers and helping them choose the right products for their needs. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in sales or a related field.

Agricultural Economists

Agricultural economists study the economic aspects of farming, including the costs and benefits of different farming techniques. They also analyze the impact of government policies on the agricultural sector. To be eligible for this job, one needs to have a degree in economics or a related field.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the agricultural sector in Zambia is growing, leading to the creation of new job opportunities. The jobs mentioned above are just a few examples of the latest agriculture jobs in Zambia in 2023. If you are interested in pursuing a career in agriculture, there are many options available to you.
