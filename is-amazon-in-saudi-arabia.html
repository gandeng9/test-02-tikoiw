The Rise of E-commerce in Saudi Arabia

E-commerce has been on the rise in Saudi Arabia in recent years, with more and more people turning to online shopping for their needs. This has led to the emergence of several e-commerce websites in the country, including Souq.com, noon.com, and Amazon.sa.

Amazon's Entry into Saudi Arabia

Amazon entered the Saudi Arabian market in 2019, after acquiring Souq.com, which was the largest e-commerce platform in the Middle East. The acquisition allowed Amazon to expand its presence in the region and tap into the growing e-commerce market in Saudi Arabia.
