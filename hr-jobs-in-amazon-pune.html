Job Opportunities

Amazon Pune offers a wide range of HR job opportunities that cater to different levels of expertise. These include HR Business Partner, Talent Acquisition Specialist, HR Operations Associate, and L&D Program Manager. Each of these roles requires specific skills and qualifications that candidates must possess.

HR Business Partner

As an HR Business Partner, the candidate must have a bachelor's degree in HR or related field with at least five years of experience in HR. The candidate must have strong communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills, as well as the ability to work in a team environment. The HR Business Partner will work closely with the business leaders to develop and implement HR strategies that align with the company's goals.

Talent Acquisition Specialist

The Talent Acquisition Specialist is responsible for sourcing, screening, and hiring top talent for Amazon Pune. The candidate must have a bachelor's degree in HR or related field with at least three years of experience in recruitment. The candidate must have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, as well as the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

HR Operations Associate

The HR Operations Associate is responsible for managing the HR processes and systems. The candidate must have a bachelor's degree in HR or related field with at least two years of experience in HR operations. The candidate must have strong attention to detail and organizational skills, as well as the ability to work in a team environment.

L&D Program Manager

The L&D Program Manager is responsible for designing, developing, and implementing learning and development programs for Amazon Pune employees. The candidate must have a bachelor's degree in HR or related field with at least five years of experience in L&D. The candidate must have excellent communication and project management skills, as well as the ability to work independently.

Requirements

All HR job positions in Amazon Pune require a bachelor's degree in HR or related field.
