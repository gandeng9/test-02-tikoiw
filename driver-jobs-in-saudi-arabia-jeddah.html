Driver Jobs In Saudi Arabia Jeddah

Introduction

If you are looking for driver jobs in Saudi Arabia, Jeddah is a great place to start. With its booming economy and growing population, there is a high demand for drivers in the city. Whether you are a local or an expat, there are many job opportunities available for drivers in Jeddah.

Types of Driver Jobs

Private Drivers

Private drivers are hired by individuals or families to provide transportation services. They usually work for a single employer and are responsible for driving the employer and their family members to various locations. Private drivers must have a valid driver's license and a clean driving record.

Taxi Drivers

Taxi drivers provide transportation services to the general public. They work for taxi companies or operate their own taxi business. Taxi drivers must have a valid driver's license and a commercial driving license.

Delivery Drivers

Delivery drivers are responsible for transporting goods and packages from one location to another. They work for courier companies, retail stores, and other businesses. Delivery drivers must have a valid driver's license and a clean driving record.

Requirements for Driver Jobs

To work as a driver in Jeddah, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Valid driver's license
  • Clean driving record
  • Fluent in Arabic or English
  • Good communication skills
  • Knowledge of the city's roads and traffic laws

Salary and Benefits

The salary for driver jobs in Jeddah varies depending on the type of job and the employer. Private drivers usually earn more than taxi or delivery drivers. The average salary for a driver in Jeddah is around SAR 4,000 per month.

Employers may provide additional benefits such as health insurance, paid vacation, and bonuses.

How to Find Driver Jobs in Jeddah

There are several ways to find driver jobs in Jeddah:

  • Online job portals
  • Newspaper classifieds
  • Referrals from friends or family members
  • Directly contacting taxi or courier companies

Conclusion

If you are looking for a job as a driver in Jeddah, there are many opportunities available. Make sure you meet the requirements and have the necessary skills to be successful in this field. With hard work and dedication, you can have a fulfilling career as a driver in Jeddah.
