Part Time Jobs in Bangalore Weekend - 2023

Introduction

Are you looking for part time jobs in Bangalore on weekends? You have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss some great opportunities for you to earn some extra money while pursuing your hobbies and interests.

1. Tutoring

Requirements

If you have expertise in any subject, you can become a tutor. All you need is good communication skills and patience to teach others. You can find students through online platforms or by spreading the word through your friends and family.

Earnings

You can earn anywhere between Rs. 500 to Rs. 2,000 per hour depending on your expertise and experience.

2. Event Management

Requirements

If you have a creative mind and love to plan events, you can become an event manager. You need to have good communication skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work under pressure. You can find clients through social media or by networking with people in your circle.

Earnings

You can earn anywhere between Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 50,000 per event depending on the scale and complexity of the event.

3. Freelance Writing

Requirements

If you have a flair for writing, you can become a freelance writer. You need to have good writing skills, creativity, and the ability to meet deadlines. You can find clients through online platforms or by sending pitches to publications.

Earnings

You can earn anywhere between Rs. 1 to Rs. 5 per word depending on your experience and the type of content you write.

4. Pet Care

Requirements

If you love animals, you can become a pet care provider. You need to have a basic understanding of pet care and the ability to handle different types of animals. You can find clients through social media or by advertising in your local community.

Earnings

You can earn anywhere between Rs. 500 to Rs. 2,000 per day depending on the type of service you provide.

5. Photography

Requirements

If you have a passion for photography, you can become a freelance photographer. You need to have good photography skills, creativity, and the ability to work with different types of clients. You can find clients through social media or by creating a portfolio and showcasing your work.

Earnings

You can earn anywhere between Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 50,000 per project depending on the type of photography and the scale of the project.

6. Food Delivery

Requirements

If you have a two-wheeler and a smartphone, you can become a food delivery executive. You need to have good communication skills and the ability to navigate through the city. You can find opportunities through food delivery apps such as Swiggy, Zomato, and Uber Eats.

Earnings

You can earn anywhere between Rs. 100 to Rs. 500 per delivery depending on the distance and the order value.

Conclusion

There are plenty of opportunities for part-time jobs in Bangalore on weekends. You just need to explore your interests and skills and find the right opportunity for you. We hope this article has given you some ideas and inspiration to pursue your dreams.
