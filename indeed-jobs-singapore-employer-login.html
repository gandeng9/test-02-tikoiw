How To Use Indeed Jobs Singapore Employer Login In 2023

Introduction

As an employer in Singapore, you are always looking for the best talent to fill your vacancies. One of the best places to find qualified candidates is on Indeed Jobs Singapore. This job board is one of the most popular in the country, attracting millions of job seekers every year. In this article, we will show you how to use the Indeed Jobs Singapore Employer Login to find the best candidates for your vacancies.

Register for an Employer Account

The first step to using Indeed Jobs Singapore as an employer is to register for an employer account. You can do this by visiting the Indeed Singapore website and clicking on the "Employer Login" button. From there, you will be prompted to create an account by entering your email address and creating a password. Once you have created your account, you can start posting job vacancies and reviewing resumes.

Posting Job Vacancies

Posting job vacancies on Indeed Jobs Singapore is easy. After logging in to your employer account, click on "Post a Job" and fill out the necessary information, including job title, job description, and salary range. You can also include any necessary qualifications and experience required for the position. Once you have filled out all the information, click "Post Job" to make your vacancy live on the site.

Reviewing Resumes

One of the best features of Indeed Jobs Singapore is the ability to review resumes from job seekers who have applied for your vacancies. You can easily access resumes by logging in to your employer account and clicking on "My Jobs." From there, you can view all resumes submitted for your vacancies and filter by qualifications and experience.

Using the Resume Search Feature

If you are not finding the right candidates for your vacancies, you can use the Indeed Jobs Singapore resume search feature. This feature allows you to search for resumes of job seekers who have not applied for your vacancies. You can use various filters such as job title, industry, and location to narrow down your search results.

Managing Your Job Listings

As an employer, you will want to keep track of all your job listings and their progress. Indeed Jobs Singapore allows you to manage all your job listings from your employer account. You can view how many views and applications each vacancy has received and edit or delete your postings as necessary.

Using Sponsored Job Listings

If you want to increase the visibility of your job vacancies on Indeed Jobs Singapore, you can use the sponsored job listings feature. This feature allows you to promote your vacancies to a wider audience by placing them at the top of search results. You can set a budget for your sponsored listings and track their performance.

Conclusion

Indeed Jobs Singapore is a powerful tool for employers looking to find the best candidates for their vacancies. By using the employer login feature, you can post job vacancies, review resumes, and search for qualified candidates. With the resume search feature and sponsored job listings, you can further enhance your chances of finding the right candidate for your positions. Start using Indeed Jobs Singapore today and take your recruitment efforts to the next level.
