If you're looking for job opportunities in Dallas, Oregon, you're in luck! 1. Amazon

Amazon is one of the largest companies in the world, and they've recently opened a new fulfillment center in Dallas, Oregon. This facility is expected to create hundreds of new jobs in the area, including positions in warehouse operations, logistics, and management.

2. Walmart

Walmart is another major employer in Dallas, Oregon. The company has a large retail store in the city, as well as a distribution center that serves the Pacific Northwest region. Walmart is always hiring for a variety of roles, from cashiers and sales associates to truck drivers and warehouse workers.

3. Polk County School District

If you're interested in working in education, the Polk County School District is a great place to start. The district serves several communities in the area, including Dallas, and offers a range of teaching and support positions. Whether you're a certified teacher or a paraeducator, there are opportunities to make a difference in the lives of local students.

4. Salem Health

Salem Health is a leading healthcare provider in the region, with several facilities in and around Dallas. The organization is always looking for talented medical professionals, including nurses, doctors, and support staff. Salem Health also offers competitive salaries and comprehensive benefits packages.

5. Spirit Mountain Casino

Spirit Mountain Casino is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike, and it's also a major employer in the area. The casino offers a wide range of jobs, from dealers and servers to security officers and marketing specialists. If you're looking for a fun and exciting work environment, Spirit Mountain Casino might be the perfect fit.

6. Oregon State Police

The Oregon State Police is responsible for enforcing the law and providing public safety services throughout the state. The agency has a local office in Dallas, and they're always looking for qualified candidates to join their team. Whether you're interested in becoming a trooper or a forensic scientist, the Oregon State Police offers a range of career paths.

7. Home Depot

Home Depot is a leading home improvement retailer, and they have a store in Dallas that serves the local community. The company is always hiring for a variety of roles, from sales associates and cashiers to managers and supervisors. 8. Pacific Foods

Pacific Foods is a leading producer of organic and natural foods, with a manufacturing facility in Dallas. The company is known for their high-quality products and commitment to sustainability, and they're always looking for talented individuals to join their team. Whether you're interested in production, quality control, or research and development, Pacific Foods has a variety of job opportunities available.

9. Walmart Distribution Center

In addition to their retail store in Dallas, Walmart also has a distribution center in the area. 10. Polk County Government

Finally, the Polk County government is another major employer in the area. The county offers a range of services to local residents, including law enforcement, public works, and social services. Whether you're a recent graduate, a seasoned professional, or somewhere in between, Dallas, Oregon has plenty of job opportunities to offer.
