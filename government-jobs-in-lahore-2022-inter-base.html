Government Jobs In Lahore For Intermediate Pass Students In 2022

Introduction

Are you an intermediate pass student looking for a government job in Lahore? If yes, then you are in the right place. Lahore is a city of opportunities and there are plenty of government jobs available for intermediate pass students. In this article, we will discuss some of the most sought-after government jobs in Lahore that you can apply for in 2022.

Police Jobs

The police department is one of the largest employers in Lahore. They offer a variety of jobs for intermediate pass students such as constables, drivers, and clerks. To apply for these jobs, you need to meet certain criteria such as age, height, and physical fitness. The selection process includes a written test, physical test, and interview.

Education Department Jobs

The education department of Lahore also offers jobs for intermediate pass students. These jobs include teaching and non-teaching staff such as clerks, lab attendants, and peons. To apply for these jobs, you need to have good communication skills and basic computer knowledge. The selection process includes a written test and interview.

Health Department Jobs

The health department of Lahore also offers jobs for intermediate pass students. These jobs include nurses, midwives, and lab technicians. To apply for these jobs, you need to have a diploma or certificate in the relevant field. The selection process includes a written test and interview.

Revenue Department Jobs

The revenue department of Lahore also offers jobs for intermediate pass students. These jobs include patwaris, clerks, and data entry operators. To apply for these jobs, you need to have good typing speed and computer knowledge. The selection process includes a written test and interview.

Transport Department Jobs

The transport department of Lahore also offers jobs for intermediate pass students. These jobs include drivers, conductors, and clerks. To apply for these jobs, you need to have a valid driving license and basic computer knowledge. The selection process includes a written test and interview.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are plenty of government job opportunities available for intermediate pass students in Lahore. You just need to stay updated with the latest job openings and apply accordingly. Make sure to fulfill all the criteria and prepare well for the selection process. We hope this article has provided you with some valuable insights about government jobs in Lahore. Good luck with your job search!
