Discover The Best Places To Shop In Little Rock In 2023

Introduction

Are you planning a trip to Little Rock, Arkansas in 2023? If you are, then you should know that Little Rock is a fantastic place to shop. With a wide range of stores, boutiques, and malls, you'll never run out of options. In this article, we'll explore the top places to shop in Little Rock in 2023.

The Promenade at Chenal

If you're looking for a high-end shopping experience, then The Promenade at Chenal is the place to go. This outdoor mall features a variety of upscale stores, including Pottery Barn, Williams Sonoma, and Sephora. You'll also find a wide range of dining options, making it the perfect place to spend an afternoon.

The Outlets at Little Rock

For those who love a good bargain, The Outlets at Little Rock is a must-visit. This outlet mall features over 70 stores, including Nike, Polo Ralph Lauren, and Kate Spade. You can expect to find discounts of up to 70% off retail prices, making it the perfect place to score a deal.

Hillcrest

If you're looking for a more eclectic shopping experience, then Hillcrest is the place to go. This historic neighborhood is home to a variety of independent shops and boutiques, including E. Leigh's Boutique and Box Turtle. You'll also find a variety of dining options, making it the perfect place to spend a day.

The River Market

For a unique shopping experience, head to The River Market. This open-air market features a variety of vendors selling everything from fresh produce to handmade crafts. You'll also find a variety of restaurants and cafes, making it the perfect place to grab a bite to eat.

Park Plaza Mall

Park Plaza Mall is another great option for those who love to shop. This indoor mall features a variety of stores, including Dillard's, J.C. Penney, and Forever 21. You'll also find a variety of dining options, making it the perfect place to spend a day with friends.

Midtown

If you're looking for a more upscale shopping experience, then Midtown is the place to go. This neighborhood is home to a variety of high-end stores, including Anthropologie, Lululemon, and Kendra Scott. You'll also find a variety of dining options, making it the perfect place to spend an afternoon.

Little Rock Farmer's Market

For fresh, locally grown produce, head to the Little Rock Farmer's Market. This market is open on Saturdays from May to October and features a variety of vendors selling everything from fruits and vegetables to handmade crafts.

Conclusion

As you can see, Little Rock is a fantastic place to shop. Whether you're looking for high-end stores, bargain shopping, or a unique shopping experience, you'll find it all in Little Rock. So start planning your trip today and discover all the great places to shop in Little Rock in 2023!
