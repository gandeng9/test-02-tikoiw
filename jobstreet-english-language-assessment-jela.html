Everything You Need To Know About Jobstreet English Language Assessment (Jela)

Introduction

JobStreet is one of the most popular online job portals in Southeast Asia. It provides a platform for job seekers to find employment opportunities and for recruiters to post job openings. In 2023, JobStreet introduced a new feature called the English Language Assessment (JELA) test.

What is JELA?

JELA is an English proficiency test designed to evaluate the language skills of job seekers. It is a standardized test that is used to measure a candidate's ability to read, write, listen, and speak in English. The test is conducted online and is available for free to all JobStreet members.

Why is JELA Important?

English is the most widely spoken language in the world, and it is also the language of business. Many companies in Southeast Asia require their employees to have a good command of English, especially in multinational corporations. JELA helps recruiters assess the English language proficiency of job seekers and make informed hiring decisions.

How Does JELA Work?

The JELA test consists of four sections: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. The test is adaptive, meaning that the difficulty level of each question adjusts based on the candidate's responses. The test takes approximately 60 minutes to complete, and the results are available immediately after the test.

Who Can Take JELA?

JELA is available to all JobStreet members who want to assess their English language proficiency. However, it is recommended for job seekers who are applying for positions that require good English communication skills.

How to Prepare for JELA?

JobStreet provides a JELA practice test to help candidates prepare for the actual test. The practice test is designed to give candidates an idea of the types of questions they can expect in the actual test. Candidates can also improve their English language skills by reading English books, watching English movies, and practicing speaking with native English speakers.

Benefits of Taking JELA

JELA provides several benefits to job seekers, including:

  • Assessing their English language proficiency level
  • Identifying areas where they need to improve their language skills
  • Increasing their chances of getting hired in companies that require good English communication skills

Conclusion

JELA is an important tool for job seekers who want to assess their English language proficiency level. It helps recruiters make informed hiring decisions and increases the chances of job seekers getting hired in companies that require good English communication skills. JobStreet members can take the JELA test for free and prepare for the test using the JELA practice test provided by JobStreet.
