Jobs In Orlando That Pay $20 An Hour

Introduction

Finding a well-paying job is a dream for every job seeker. In Orlando, there are many job opportunities that pay $20 an hour or more. These jobs are available in various industries, from healthcare to technology. In this article, we will explore some of the jobs in Orlando that pay $20 an hour in 2023.

Healthcare Jobs

The healthcare industry is one of the fastest-growing industries in Orlando. If you have a passion for helping people, you can consider a career in healthcare. There are many healthcare jobs that pay $20 an hour or more, such as medical assistants, registered nurses, and surgical technologists. Medical assistants are responsible for performing administrative and clinical tasks in healthcare settings. They earn an average hourly wage of $22. Registered nurses, on the other hand, provide and coordinate patient care. They earn an average hourly wage of $30. Surgical technologists assist in surgical operations and earn an average hourly wage of $24.

Technology Jobs

The technology industry is also booming in Orlando. If you have a passion for technology, you can consider a career in this industry. There are many technology jobs that pay $20 an hour or more, such as software developers, network administrators, and IT support specialists. Software developers are responsible for designing, developing and maintaining software systems. They earn an average hourly wage of $39. Network administrators, on the other hand, are responsible for maintaining computer networks. They earn an average hourly wage of $28. IT support specialists provide technical support to computer users. They earn an average hourly wage of $23.

Manufacturing Jobs

The manufacturing industry is also thriving in Orlando. If you have an interest in manufacturing, you can consider a career in this industry. There are many manufacturing jobs that pay $20 an hour or more, such as production supervisors, machinists, and assemblers. Production supervisors are responsible for managing the production process. They earn an average hourly wage of $28. Machinists, on the other hand, operate and maintain machines. They earn an average hourly wage of $23. Assemblers are responsible for assembling parts to make a finished product. They earn an average hourly wage of $20.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many job opportunities in Orlando that pay $20 an hour or more. These jobs are available in various industries, from healthcare to technology. If you are looking for a well-paying job in Orlando, consider exploring the healthcare, technology, and manufacturing industries. With the right skills and qualifications, you can land a job that pays $20 an hour or more.
