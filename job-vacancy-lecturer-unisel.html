Job Vacancy For Lecturers At Unisel

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

UNISEL is currently seeking qualified and passionate individuals to join our team as lecturers. As one of the leading higher education institutions in Malaysia, we are committed to providing our students with quality education and a conducive learning environment. If you have a passion for teaching and sharing knowledge, this might be the opportunity for you.

Requirements

To be eligible for the position of lecturer at UNISEL, you must have a Master’s degree or a Ph.D. in a relevant field. You must also have at least two years of teaching experience at the tertiary level. Additionally, you must possess good communication and interpersonal skills to effectively engage with students and colleagues.

Responsibilities

As a lecturer at UNISEL, you will be responsible for developing and delivering course materials, conducting assessments, and providing guidance and support to students. You will also be required to participate in research activities and contribute to the development of the department and institution.

Benefits

At UNISEL, we value our employees and offer a range of benefits including medical and dental coverage, professional development opportunities, and a competitive salary package. We also provide a supportive and inclusive work environment that encourages innovation and creativity.

Application Process

To apply for the position of lecturer at UNISEL, please submit your resume, cover letter, and relevant academic transcripts to the Human Resources Department. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview and teaching demonstration.

Conclusion

UNISEL is committed to providing our students with the best possible education, and we believe that our lecturers play a vital role in achieving this goal. If you are passionate about teaching and are looking for a challenging and rewarding career, we encourage you to apply for this position.

Don't miss this opportunity to join one of Malaysia's leading higher education institutions!
