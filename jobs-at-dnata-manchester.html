Ground Handlers

Ground handlers are responsible for loading and unloading luggage, cargo, and mail from planes. They also marshal aircraft, operate ground service equipment, and perform other tasks to ensure the safe and efficient operation of the airport. If you are physically fit and can work in a fast-paced environment, this job might be suitable for you.

Ramp Agents

Ramp agents are responsible for marshaling aircraft, towing, and pushing back aircraft, and ensuring that the aircraft is loaded and unloaded correctly. They also perform safety checks to ensure that the aircraft is ready for departure. If you are interested in this job, you should have good communication skills and be able to work well in a team.

Cargo Agents

Cargo agents are responsible for the efficient handling of cargo, including the loading and unloading of cargo from aircraft. They also ensure that the cargo is labeled, weighed, and documented correctly. If you have good organizational skills and attention to detail, this job might be suitable for you.
