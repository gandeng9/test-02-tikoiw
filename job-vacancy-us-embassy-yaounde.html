What is the US Embassy in Yaounde?

The US Embassy in Yaounde is the diplomatic mission of the United States of America to Cameroon. It is responsible for promoting US interests in Cameroon and providing consular services to US citizens in the country.

Working at the US Embassy in Yaounde can be a rewarding experience. In addition, the Embassy offers competitive salaries and benefits packages to its employees.
