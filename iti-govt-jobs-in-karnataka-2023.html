Introduction

In today's world, everyone wants a secure and stable job. The government sector is the most sought-after sector for job seekers. ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023 are a great opportunity for job seekers who have completed their ITI course. Karnataka is a state that offers many job opportunities in the government sector for ITI graduates.

Overview of ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023

The Karnataka government has announced many ITI Govt Jobs for the year 2023. These job opportunities are available in various departments such as Public Works Department, Water Resource Department, and Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation.

Public Works Department

The Public Works Department has announced many job opportunities for ITI graduates in Karnataka. These jobs are available for various posts such as Mechanic, Electrician, and Fitter. The selected candidates will be responsible for the maintenance and repair work of the department's machinery and equipment.

Water Resource Department

The Water Resource Department has also announced many job opportunities for ITI graduates in Karnataka. These jobs are available for various posts such as Pump Operator, Electrician, and Fitter. The selected candidates will be responsible for the maintenance and repair work of the department's machinery and equipment.

Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation

The Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation has announced job opportunities for ITI graduates in various posts such as Mechanic, Electrician, and Fitter. The selected candidates will be responsible for the maintenance and repair work of the department's buses and other vehicles.

Eligibility Criteria for ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023

To apply for ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023, candidates must have completed their ITI course from a recognized institute. They should also have a certificate of apprenticeship training from a recognized institute. Candidates must be between 18 and 35 years of age to apply for these jobs.

Application Process for ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023

The application process for ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023 is online. Candidates can visit the official website of the respective departments and fill in the application form. They must upload their educational certificates and other required documents. Candidates must also pay the application fee online.

Selection Process for ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023

The selection process for ITI Govt Jobs in Karnataka 2023 involves a written exam and a personal interview. Candidates who qualify for the written examination will be called for the personal interview. The final selection will be based on the candidate's performance in the written exam and personal interview.
