What is Phlebotomy?

Phlebotomy is the process of drawing blood from patients for medical tests, transfusions, donations, or research. Phlebotomists are trained professionals who perform this procedure with precision and care. They also label and store the blood samples for further analysis.

Requirements for Becoming a Phlebotomist in Zephyrhills

To become a phlebotomist in Zephyrhills, you must have a high school diploma or equivalent. You also need to complete a phlebotomy training program from an accredited institution. The program should include both classroom instruction and hands-on training. After completing the program, you need to pass a certification exam to become a licensed phlebotomist.
