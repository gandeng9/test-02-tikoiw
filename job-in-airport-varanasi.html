Job Opportunities In Airport Varanasi

Introduction

If you are looking for a job in the aviation industry, then Varanasi airport could be a great place to start. The airport is located in Uttar Pradesh and is one of the busiest airports in Northern India. With more than 50 airlines operating from this airport, there are plenty of job opportunities available.

Job Roles

There are several job roles available at the airport. These include ground staff, cabin crew, pilots, and air traffic controllers. The salaries for these jobs vary based on the position and experience level.

Ground Staff

Ground staff at the airport are responsible for ensuring the smooth running of operations on the ground. This includes baggage handling, check-in, security, and customer service. Ground staff can earn between INR 15,000 and INR 25,000 per month.

Cabin Crew

Cabin crew are responsible for the safety and comfort of passengers during flights. They also provide customer service and ensure that all safety protocols are followed. Cabin crew can earn between INR 25,000 and INR 50,000 per month.

Pilots

Pilots are responsible for flying the aircraft and ensuring the safety of passengers and crew. Pilots can earn between INR 1 lakh and INR 3 lakhs per month.

Air Traffic Controllers

Air traffic controllers are responsible for directing the movement of aircraft on the ground and in the air. They ensure that all aircraft are following the correct flight paths and maintain safe distances between them. Air traffic controllers can earn between INR 50,000 and INR 1 lakh per month.

Qualifications Required

To work at the airport, you will need to have the required qualifications and certifications. For ground staff and cabin crew, a high school diploma is usually sufficient. For pilots, you will need to have a commercial pilot license (CPL) and several hours of flying experience. For air traffic controllers, you will need to have completed an air traffic control course.

Benefits of Working at the Airport

Working at the airport can be an exciting and rewarding career. Some of the benefits of working at the airport include:
  • Opportunities for career progression
  • Travel benefits
  • Competitive salaries
  • Flexible work schedules
  • Working in a dynamic and fast-paced environment

Conclusion

If you are interested in a career in aviation, then working at the airport in Varanasi could be a great place to start. With a variety of job roles available and competitive salaries, there are plenty of opportunities to advance your career in this exciting industry.
