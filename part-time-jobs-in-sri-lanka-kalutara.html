1. Tutoring

Tutoring is a popular part-time job in Kalutara, especially for students. If you are good at a subject, you can offer your services to local students who need help. You can also advertise your services online through social media or tutoring websites.

2. Retail Sales

Retail sales is another popular part-time job in Kalutara. Many shops and supermarkets in the area hire part-time staff to help with sales during peak times. This job requires good communication skills and a friendly personality.

Delivery driving is a great part-time job for those who own a vehicle. Many restaurants and delivery companies in Kalutara are looking for part-time drivers to deliver food and other items. This job requires a valid driving license and good time management skills.

If you have a talent for writing, you can consider freelance writing as a part-time job. Many companies and individuals in Kalutara are looking for content writers for their websites and social media accounts. This job requires good writing skills and the ability to meet deadlines.

Kalutara is a popular destination for weddings and other events. Many event management companies in the area are looking for part-time staff to help with setup, coordination, and other tasks. This job requires good communication skills and the ability to work under pressure.

7. Social Media Management

If you are social media savvy, you can consider social media management as a part-time job. Many businesses in Kalutara are looking for social media managers to manage their online presence. This job requires good communication skills and knowledge of social media platforms.

Kalutara is a beautiful location with many scenic spots. If you have a passion for photography, you can offer your services as a part-time photographer. Many individuals and businesses in the area are looking for photographers for events and other occasions.

10. Housekeeping

Housekeeping is a popular part-time job in Kalutara, especially for women. Many households in the area are looking for part-time housekeepers to help with cleaning and other household chores. This job requires good cleaning skills and attention to detail.
