Introduction

Are you looking for a new job opportunity? If you have a passion for hospitality and golf, Baltimore Country Club might have the perfect job for you! Job Opportunities

Baltimore Country Club is currently seeking applicants for various positions, including servers, bartenders, line cooks, golf course maintenance workers, and more. The Club is looking for individuals who are passionate about delivering excellent customer service and have experience working in a fast-paced environment.

Servers and Bartenders

Servers and bartenders are responsible for providing exceptional service to members and guests. They must have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, as well as the ability to work in a team environment. Previous experience in a fine dining or hospitality environment is preferred.

Line Cooks

Line cooks are responsible for preparing food items for the Club's various restaurants and events. They must have experience working in a high-volume kitchen and be able to work under pressure. The Club is looking for individuals who are passionate about cooking and have a strong work ethic.

Golf Course Maintenance Workers

Golf course maintenance workers are responsible for maintaining the Club's golf course and grounds. They must have experience working with golf course equipment and be knowledgeable about golf course maintenance practices. The Club is looking for individuals who are passionate about golf and have a strong work ethic.

Benefits

Baltimore Country Club offers a range of benefits to its employees, including health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off, and employee discounts. The Club also provides training and development opportunities to help employees grow in their careers.

To apply for a job at Baltimore Country Club, interested individuals should visit the Club's website and submit an application online. Applicants should include a resume and cover letter detailing their experience and qualifications.

Baltimore Country Club is a great place to work for individuals who are passionate about hospitality and golf.
