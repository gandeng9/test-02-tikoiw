Steve Jobs Film 2013 Netflix: A Must-Watch Biopic

The Storyline

Steve Jobs, the mastermind behind Apple Inc., is one of the most iconic figures in the tech industry. The Steve Jobs film, released in 2013, is a biopic that portrays the life of Steve Jobs and his journey towards success. The movie is based on the biography of Steve Jobs written by Walter Isaacson. The film is directed by Danny Boyle and written by Aaron Sorkin.

The movie revolves around three major product launches of Apple Inc. – Macintosh, NeXT, and iMac. The film takes us through the ups and downs in the life of Steve Jobs and how he overcame all the obstacles to become one of the most successful entrepreneurs in the world.

The Cast

The cast of the movie is exceptional. Michael Fassbender plays the role of Steve Jobs, and he does an excellent job of portraying the character. Kate Winslet plays the role of Joanna Hoffman, who was the marketing chief of Macintosh. Jeff Daniels plays the role of John Sculley, who was the CEO of Apple Inc. during Steve Jobs' tenure. Seth Rogen plays the role of Steve Wozniak, who was the co-founder of Apple Inc.

All the actors have done an excellent job of bringing their characters to life. The chemistry between Michael Fassbender and Kate Winslet is fantastic, and they complement each other's performances very well.

The Direction and Screenplay

The direction and screenplay of the movie are top-notch. Danny Boyle has done an excellent job of bringing the story to life. The movie is shot in a unique way, and the use of flashbacks adds depth to the story. Aaron Sorkin's screenplay is brilliant, and it captures the essence of Steve Jobs and his personality.

The movie is fast-paced, and the dialogues are sharp and witty. The movie keeps you engaged from the beginning till the end.

The Music

The music of the movie is composed by Daniel Pemberton. The music is fantastic, and it complements the movie very well. The music adds to the overall experience of watching the movie.

The Reception

The movie was well-received by critics and audiences alike. The movie has a rating of 7.2/10 on IMDb and 86% on Rotten Tomatoes. The movie was nominated for two Oscars, including Best Actor for Michael Fassbender.

Conclusion

The Steve Jobs film is a must-watch biopic for anyone who is interested in the tech industry or entrepreneurship. The movie is a brilliant portrayal of the life of Steve Jobs and his journey towards success. The movie has an excellent cast, direction, screenplay, and music. The movie was well-received by critics and audiences alike, and it is definitely worth watching.

So, if you haven't watched the Steve Jobs film yet, log on to Netflix and start watching it today!
