Best Serving Jobs In Tampa In 2023

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Are you looking for the best serving jobs in Tampa? The food and beverage industry is one of the most thriving sectors in the city. With the growing demand for excellent food and customer service, there are plenty of opportunities for individuals who want to pursue a career in serving. Whether you are a student, a recent graduate, or someone who is looking to switch careers, Tampa has a lot to offer. In this article, we will discuss the best serving jobs in Tampa that you can consider in 2023.

1. Server at a High-End Restaurant

If you have experience in fine dining, then serving at a high-end restaurant in Tampa is an excellent option for you. These restaurants offer an upscale atmosphere, and their clientele expects impeccable service. As a server, you will be responsible for taking orders, serving food, and ensuring that the customers have a memorable experience. Some of the best high-end restaurants in Tampa include Bern's Steak House, Mise en Place, and Armani's.

2. Bartender

Bartending is another popular serving job in Tampa. As a bartender, you will be responsible for preparing and serving drinks to customers. You will also be expected to engage with customers and create a welcoming atmosphere. Bartending jobs are available in various settings, including restaurants, bars, and nightclubs. In Tampa, some of the best places to work as a bartender include Ciro's Speakeasy and Supper Club, The Hub Bar, and The Bricks.

3. Server at a Sports Bar

Tampa is a sports-loving city, and many people enjoy watching games while having a drink and some food. If you enjoy the fast-paced environment of a sports bar, serving at one can be an excellent option for you. As a server, you will be responsible for taking orders and serving food and drinks to customers. Some of the best sports bars in Tampa include Hattricks, Yeoman's Cask and Lion, and Beef O'Brady's.

4. Catering Server

If you enjoy working in different settings and meeting new people, then working as a catering server can be a great option for you. Catering servers work at events such as weddings, corporate parties, and other social gatherings. As a catering server, you will be responsible for setting up the event, serving food and drinks, and cleaning up afterward. Some of the best catering companies in Tampa include Puff 'n Stuff Catering, Saltblock Catering, and Olympia Catering.

5. Server at a Hotel

Tampa is a popular tourist destination, and there are many hotels in the city that offer excellent dining options. If you enjoy working in a hotel environment, then serving at one can be a great option for you. As a server, you will be responsible for taking orders and serving food to hotel guests. Some of the best hotels to work as a server in Tampa include The Ritz-Carlton, Grand Hyatt Tampa Bay, and The Vinoy Renaissance St. Petersburg Resort.

6. Food Truck Server

Food trucks have become increasingly popular in Tampa, and many of them offer delicious food options. If you enjoy working in a mobile setting and interacting with customers, then working as a food truck server can be an excellent option for you. As a food truck server, you will be responsible for taking orders, preparing food, and serving customers. Some of the best food trucks in Tampa include The Taco Traveler, Holy Hog Barbecue, and Mr. C's Grilled Cheese.

7. Server at a Cafe

If you enjoy working in a relaxed environment and serving coffee and baked goods, then working at a cafe can be an excellent option for you. As a cafe server, you will be responsible for taking orders, preparing food and drinks, and ensuring that customers have a pleasant experience. Some of the best cafes in Tampa include Buddy Brew Coffee, Oxford Exchange, and Kahwa Coffee.

8. Fine Dining Server on a Yacht

Tampa Bay is known for its beautiful waterways, and many companies offer fine dining experiences on yachts. If you enjoy working in a unique setting and providing exceptional service, then working as a fine dining server on a yacht can be a great option for you. Some of the best yacht companies in Tampa include Yacht StarShip, Tampa Bay Yacht Charter, and Tampa Bay Fun Boat.

9. Server at a Theme Park

Tampa is home to some of the best theme parks in the country, including Busch Gardens and Adventure Island. If you enjoy working in a fast-paced, exciting environment, then serving at a theme park can be a great option for you. As a server, you will be responsible for taking orders and serving food and drinks to guests. Some of the best theme park restaurants in Tampa include Serengeti Overlook Restaurant, Zagora Cafe, and Mango Joe's.

10. Server at a Brewery

Tampa is home to many breweries that offer delicious craft beer and food. If you enjoy working in a fun, laid-back environment, then serving at a brewery can be an excellent option for you. As a server, you will be responsible for taking orders and serving food and drinks to customers. Some of the best breweries to work at in Tampa include Cigar City Brewing, Coppertail Brewing Co., and Angry Chair Brewing.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many excellent serving jobs available in Tampa. Whether you prefer working in a fine dining restaurant, a sports bar, or a theme park, there is something for everyone. Consider your interests, skills, and experience when deciding which serving job to pursue. With hard work and dedication, you can have a successful serving career in Tampa.
