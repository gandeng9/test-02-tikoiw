Introduction

With the ever-growing demand for tech skills in the job market, coding bootcamps have become a popular option for individuals looking to upskill or switch careers. However, not everyone can commit to full-time bootcamps due to work or personal commitments. This is where part-time coding bootcamps come in. In this article, we will be discussing the top 10 best part-time coding bootcamps in 2023.

What are Part-Time Coding Bootcamps?

Part-time coding bootcamps are programs that offer the same curriculum as full-time bootcamps, but with a more flexible schedule. These bootcamps typically run for a longer period of time, ranging from 6 to 12 months, and are designed to be completed alongside a full-time job or other commitments.

1. General Assembly

General Assembly’s part-time coding bootcamp offers a flexible schedule and a comprehensive curriculum that covers full-stack web development. The program lasts for 24 weeks and includes live online classes, office hours, and project-based learning.

Conclusion

Part-time coding bootcamps offer a great opportunity for individuals to upskill or switch careers without having to sacrifice their current job or personal commitments.
