Indonesia Work From Home Visa 2023: Everything You Need To Know

Introduction

In response to the global pandemic, many countries have implemented work from home policies. Indonesia has taken it one step further by introducing a work from home visa to attract remote workers to the country.

What is the Indonesia Work from Home Visa?

The Indonesia Work from Home Visa is a new type of visa that allows foreign nationals to work remotely from Indonesia for up to 12 months.

Who is Eligible?

Foreign nationals who are employed by a company outside Indonesia and work remotely are eligible for the Work from Home Visa. Applicants must also have a minimum monthly income of USD 1,200.

How to Apply

Applicants must apply for the Work from Home Visa through the Indonesian embassy or consulate in their home country. The application process requires a valid passport, proof of employment and income, and a statement of purpose.

Benefits of the Work from Home Visa

The Work from Home Visa allows foreign nationals to experience Indonesia's unique culture and lifestyle while working remotely. Additionally, the visa provides an opportunity for digital nomads to network with other remote workers and entrepreneurs in the country.

Cost of Living in Indonesia

Indonesia's cost of living is relatively low compared to other countries, making it an attractive option for remote workers. However, it is important to note that the cost of living varies depending on the city and region.

Popular Cities for Remote Workers in Indonesia

Bali

Bali is a popular destination for digital nomads due to its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and affordable cost of living.

Jakarta

Jakarta is the capital city of Indonesia and has a thriving start-up scene, making it an ideal location for remote workers looking to network and collaborate with other entrepreneurs.

Yogyakarta

Yogyakarta is a cultural hub in Indonesia and home to numerous coworking spaces, making it an excellent location for remote workers.

Conclusion

The Indonesia Work from Home Visa is an excellent opportunity for remote workers to experience Indonesia's unique culture and lifestyle while working remotely. With the low cost of living and numerous coworking spaces, Indonesia is an attractive option for digital nomads looking to network and collaborate with other remote workers and entrepreneurs.
