Vacancies In Airport Security Force

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

In this year 2023, the Airport Security Force (ASF) has announced vacancies for various positions in their department. This is a great opportunity for those who want to pursue their careers in the aviation industry. In this article, we will discuss the details of these vacancies and how to apply for them.

Positions Available

ASF has announced vacancies for the following positions: 1. Assistant Director (Legal) 2. Assistant Director (BPS-17) 3. Inspector (BPS-16) 4. Assistant (BPS-15) 5. Stenotypist (BPS-14) 6. Draftsman (BPS-11) 7. UDC (BPS-11) 8. LDC (BPS-09) 9. Technical Assistant (BPS-07) 10. Electrician (BPS-05) 11. Plumber (BPS-05) 12. Carpenter (BPS-05)

Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria for each position vary, but the basic requirements are as follows: 1. Assistant Director (Legal): LLB degree from a recognized university with 3 years of experience in the relevant field. 2. Assistant Director (BPS-17): Master's degree in a relevant field with 3 years of experience in the relevant field. 3. Inspector (BPS-16): Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. 4. Assistant (BPS-15): Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. 5. Stenotypist (BPS-14): Intermediate with shorthand and typing speed of 80/40 words per minute. 6. Draftsman (BPS-11): Matric with a diploma in Drafting and 3 years of experience in the relevant field. 7. UDC (BPS-11): Intermediate with typing speed of 30 words per minute. 8. LDC (BPS-09): Matric with typing speed of 30 words per minute. 9. Technical Assistant (BPS-07): Matric with a diploma in the relevant field. 10. Electrician (BPS-05): Matric with a diploma in Electrical and 2 years of experience in the relevant field. 11. Plumber (BPS-05): Matric with a diploma in Plumbing and 2 years of experience in the relevant field. 12. Carpenter (BPS-05): Matric with a diploma in Carpentry and 2 years of experience in the relevant field.

How to Apply

To apply for any of the above positions, visit the ASF website and download the application form. Fill out the form and attach the required documents. Submit the application form along with the application fee to the designated bank. The deadline for submission of application forms is 30th June 2023.

Benefits of Working in ASF

Working in ASF has numerous benefits, including: 1. Competitive salary packages 2. Job security 3. Health and life insurance 4. Annual leaves 5. Opportunities for growth and development 6. Modern working environment 7. Opportunities to work on a variety of projects

Conclusion

In conclusion, if you are looking for a career in the aviation industry, the vacancies in ASF are a great opportunity for you. Make sure to check the eligibility criteria before applying and submit your application before the deadline. Working in ASF offers numerous benefits and opportunities for growth and development. We wish you the best of luck in your job search.
