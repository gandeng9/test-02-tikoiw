What are Part-Time Jobs?

Part-time jobs are positions that require employees to work fewer hours than a full-time job, typically less than 35 hours per week. They can be found in a wide range of industries, including retail, hospitality, healthcare, and customer service. Part-time jobs can be a great way to earn extra income or gain experience in a new field.

There are many benefits to working part-time, including increased flexibility and a better work-life balance. Part-time jobs can also offer the opportunity to learn new skills and gain valuable experience that can be used to further your career. For students, part-time jobs can be a great way to earn money while studying, and for retirees, they can provide a way to stay active and engaged in the workforce.
