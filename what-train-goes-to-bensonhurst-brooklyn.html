Introduction

Bensonhurst is a beautiful neighborhood located in the southwestern part of Brooklyn, New York. It is a great place to visit, with its diverse culture, beautiful parks, and excellent restaurants. However, if you are new to the area, you may be wondering what train goes to Bensonhurst Brooklyn. In this article, we will discuss the different train options available to get to Bensonhurst.

The Subway System in Brooklyn

The subway system in Brooklyn is one of the most convenient ways to travel around the borough. It is affordable, efficient, and connects you to all the major neighborhoods in Brooklyn. There are several subway lines that run through Bensonhurst, making it easy to get there from any part of Brooklyn or Manhattan.

The N Train

The N train is one of the most popular trains that go to Bensonhurst Brooklyn. It runs from Astoria in Queens to Coney Island in Brooklyn, and it stops at several stations in Bensonhurst, including 18th Avenue, 20th Avenue, and Bay Parkway. The N train runs 24 hours a day, seven days a week, making it a convenient option for travelers.

The D Train

Another train that goes to Bensonhurst Brooklyn is the D train. It runs from Norwood in the Bronx to Coney Island in Brooklyn, and it stops at several stations in Bensonhurst, including 20th Avenue, Bay Parkway, and 25th Avenue. The D train also runs 24 hours a day, seven days a week, making it another convenient option for travelers.

The F Train

The F train is another option to get to Bensonhurst Brooklyn. It runs from Jamaica in Queens to Coney Island in Brooklyn, and it stops at several stations in Bensonhurst, including 18th Avenue and Avenue N. The F train runs 24 hours a day, seven days a week, making it a great option for travelers.

Other Options

Aside from the subway, there are other transportation options available to get to Bensonhurst Brooklyn. You can take a taxi or rideshare service, which can be more expensive but offer more convenience. You can also take a bus, which can be slower than the subway, but it can take you to areas that the subway does not cover.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are several train options available to get to Bensonhurst Brooklyn. The N train, the D train, and the F train are the most convenient options that run through Bensonhurst. However, there are other transportation options available, including taxis, rideshares, and buses. No matter how you choose to get there, Bensonhurst is a great place to visit, with its rich culture and excellent attractions.
