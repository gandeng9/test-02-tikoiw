Airport Jobs In Kerala For Freshers In 2023

Introduction

Are you a fresh graduate looking for a job in Kerala? Do you have a passion for the aviation industry? If so, you're in luck! Kerala has several airports that provide job opportunities for freshers. In this article, we'll explore the various airport jobs available in Kerala and how you can land one.

Types of Airport Jobs

There are several types of airport jobs available in Kerala. Some of the most common jobs include ground staff, security personnel, customer service executives, air traffic controllers, and baggage handlers. Each of these jobs requires different skills and qualifications, but they all offer exciting opportunities for freshers.

Ground Staff

Ground staff are responsible for various tasks, such as checking in passengers, handling baggage, and assisting passengers with special needs. To become a ground staff member, you typically need a high school diploma or equivalent. You should also have excellent communication and customer service skills.

Security Personnel

Security personnel are responsible for ensuring the safety and security of passengers, employees, and airport property. To become a security personnel, you typically need to complete a training program and obtain a security clearance. You should also have strong observational and communication skills.

Customer Service Executives

Customer service executives are responsible for assisting passengers with their travel needs, such as booking flights, changing reservations, and providing information about airport services. To become a customer service executive, you typically need a bachelor's degree in hospitality, tourism, or a related field. You should also have excellent communication and customer service skills.

Air Traffic Controllers

Air traffic controllers are responsible for ensuring the safe and efficient movement of aircraft in and out of the airport. To become an air traffic controller, you typically need a bachelor's degree in aviation or a related field. You should also have excellent communication and problem-solving skills.

Baggage Handlers

Baggage handlers are responsible for loading and unloading luggage from aircraft. To become a baggage handler, you typically need a high school diploma or equivalent. You should also have the ability to lift heavy objects and work in a fast-paced environment.

How to Land an Airport Job

To land an airport job in Kerala, you should start by researching the various job opportunities available. You can check job boards, company websites, and social media platforms for job postings. You can also attend job fairs and networking events to meet with recruiters and hiring managers. When applying for airport jobs, make sure to tailor your resume and cover letter to the specific job you're applying for. Highlight your relevant skills and experiences, and explain why you're interested in working in the aviation industry. During the interview process, make sure to dress professionally and be prepared to answer questions about your skills and experiences. You should also ask questions about the job and the company to demonstrate your interest and enthusiasm.

Conclusion

If you're a fresh graduate looking for a job in Kerala, consider pursuing a career in the aviation industry. There are several types of airport jobs available, and each offers exciting opportunities for growth and development. By researching the job opportunities available and preparing for the application and interview process, you can land an airport job and launch your career in the aviation industry.
