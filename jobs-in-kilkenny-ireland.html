The Thriving Job Market in Kilkenny

Kilkenny is a picturesque city located in the south-east of Ireland. The city has a population of over 26,000 people and is known for its rich history, charming medieval streets, and vibrant arts and culture scene. The city is also home to a thriving job market, with a range of opportunities available in various industries.

Industries with Job Opportunities in Kilkenny

One of the major industries in Kilkenny is the technology sector, with companies such as Glanbia, Vhi Healthcare, and Taxback.com having their headquarters in the city. There are also opportunities available in the healthcare sector, with St. Luke's General Hospital and Aut Even Hospital being major employers in the area. Other industries with job opportunities in Kilkenny include retail, hospitality, and finance.

Top Employers in Kilkenny

Some of the top employers in Kilkenny include Glanbia, which is a global nutrition company that produces a range of dairy and nutritional ingredients. The company has a workforce of over 6,000 employees worldwide, with a significant number of them based in Kilkenny. Another major employer in the city is State Street, which is a leading provider of financial services to institutional investors. The company has a global workforce of over 30,000 people, with a number of them based in Kilkenny.

How to Find Job Opportunities in Kilkenny

If you are looking for job opportunities in Kilkenny, there are several resources available to you. These include online job boards such as Indeed and LinkedIn, as well as local recruitment agencies such as Collins McNicholas and Matrix Recruitment Group. You can also check the websites of major employers in the area to see if they have any job vacancies available.

Living and Working in Kilkenny

The Cost of Living in Kilkenny

The cost of living in Kilkenny is generally lower than in major cities such as Dublin and Cork. The cost of housing, food, and transportation is more affordable, making it an attractive option for those looking to relocate. However, it is important to note that the cost of living can vary depending on your lifestyle and personal circumstances.

The Lifestyle in Kilkenny

Kilkenny is a city that offers a high quality of life, with a range of activities and attractions to suit all interests. The city is known for its vibrant arts and culture scene, with a number of festivals and events taking place throughout the year. There are also plenty of outdoor activities available, including hiking, cycling, and fishing.

Getting Around Kilkenny

Kilkenny is a compact city that is easy to navigate on foot or by bicycle. There is also a reliable public transportation system, with buses running throughout the city and to surrounding areas. If you have a car, there is ample parking available in the city center and surrounding areas.

Conclusion

Overall, Kilkenny is a city that offers a range of job opportunities in various industries, as well as a high quality of life. If you are considering relocating to Ireland, Kilkenny is definitely worth considering as a place to live and work.
