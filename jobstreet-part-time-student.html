Finding Part-Time Jobs For Students On Jobstreet In 2023

Introduction

Many students nowadays are looking for part-time jobs to support their studies and daily expenses. One of the most popular job search websites in Southeast Asia is JobStreet. In this article, we will discuss how to find part-time jobs for students on JobStreet in 2023.

Creating an Account on JobStreet

To start your job search on JobStreet, you need to create an account first. You can sign up using your email address or your social media accounts such as Facebook or LinkedIn. After signing up, you can complete your profile by uploading your resume, setting up job alerts, and indicating your preferred job categories.

Tips on Creating a Winning Profile

When creating your profile on JobStreet, make sure to highlight your skills, achievements, and experiences. Use keywords that are relevant to the job you are applying for. Upload a professional-looking photo and include a brief introduction about yourself.

Searching for Part-Time Jobs

Once you have completed your profile, you can start searching for part-time jobs on JobStreet. You can use the search bar to look for specific job titles or use the filters to narrow down your search based on job category, location, salary range, and company size.

Tips on Effective Job Searching

To increase your chances of finding a part-time job that suits your qualifications and preferences, make sure to use specific keywords in your search. You can also save your searches and set up job alerts to receive notifications when new job postings that match your criteria are available.

Applying for Part-Time Jobs

When you find a part-time job that interests you, you can apply directly on JobStreet by clicking the "Apply" button. You can also save the job posting for future reference or share it with your friends who may be interested in the same job.

Tips on Writing a Winning Application

When applying for a part-time job on JobStreet, make sure to read the job description carefully and tailor your application to the requirements of the job. Write a compelling cover letter that highlights your skills and experiences that are relevant to the job. Include your updated resume and other supporting documents if required.

Following Up on Your Application

After submitting your application, you can track its status on JobStreet. You can also send a follow-up email or call the employer to inquire about the status of your application and express your interest in the job.

Tips on Effective Follow-Up

When following up on your application, make sure to be polite and professional. Express your enthusiasm for the job and ask if there are any updates or additional information that you can provide. Avoid being too pushy or demanding.

Conclusion

JobStreet is a great platform for students who are looking for part-time jobs. By creating a winning profile, using effective job searching techniques, writing a winning application, and following up on your application, you can increase your chances of landing a part-time job that suits your needs and preferences. Good luck with your job search!
