Driver Jobs In Guwahati Airport: Opportunities And Requirements In 2023

Introduction

Guwahati Airport, also known as Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, is a major airport in Assam, India. It serves the northeastern region of India and handles a significant amount of domestic and international traffic. As a result, there are many job opportunities available at the airport, including driver jobs. In this article, we will discuss the requirements and opportunities for driver jobs in Guwahati Airport in 2023.

Requirements for Driver Jobs in Guwahati Airport

To work as a driver at Guwahati Airport, you must meet certain requirements. Firstly, you must have a valid driving license issued by the Regional Transport Office (RTO). Secondly, you must have a minimum of three years of driving experience. Thirdly, you must be able to communicate in basic English or Hindi. Finally, you must be physically fit and able to work in shift duties.

Opportunities for Driver Jobs in Guwahati Airport

There are several opportunities for driver jobs at Guwahati Airport. The airport requires drivers for various purposes, including transporting passengers, cargo, and airport staff. The airport also hires drivers for emergency services, such as ambulance and fire services. Additionally, car rental companies and airlines operating at the airport require drivers for their operations.

How to Apply for Driver Jobs in Guwahati Airport

To apply for a driver job at Guwahati Airport, you can visit the official website of the airport or contact the airport's human resources department. You can also apply for driver jobs through job portals and recruitment agencies. It is essential to provide accurate and complete information in your application, including your driving license, experience, and contact details.

Salary and Benefits for Driver Jobs in Guwahati Airport

The salary for driver jobs in Guwahati Airport varies depending on the employer and the job role. Drivers working for the airport or government agencies typically earn higher salaries than those working for private companies. The average salary for a driver in Guwahati Airport is between INR 15,000 to INR 25,000 per month. Benefits for driver jobs may include health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

Tips for Getting Hired as a Driver in Guwahati Airport

To increase your chances of getting hired as a driver at Guwahati Airport, you can follow these tips:
  • Keep your driving license and documents up-to-date.
  • Gain additional driving experience by taking up part-time or temporary jobs.
  • Improve your communication skills in English or Hindi.
  • Learn about the airport's rules and regulations regarding driving and safety.
  • Dress professionally and arrive on time for interviews.

Conclusion

In conclusion, driver jobs in Guwahati Airport offer many opportunities for employment and career growth. However, it is essential to meet the requirements, apply accurately, and prepare well for interviews to get hired. With determination and the right skills, you can secure a well-paying and fulfilling job as a driver at Guwahati Airport in 2023.
