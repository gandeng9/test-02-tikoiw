The Growing Demand for X Ray Technicians in Virginia Beach

If you are looking for a career in the healthcare industry, becoming an X Ray technician could be a great option for you. With the growing demand for diagnostic imaging services across Virginia Beach, there is a significant need for skilled professionals who can operate X Ray machines and produce accurate images for physicians and other healthcare providers.

What Does an X Ray Technician Do?

X Ray technicians, also known as radiologic technologists, use X Ray machines to produce images of patients' internal structures. They work closely with physicians and other healthcare providers to ensure that the images produced are of high quality and can be used to diagnose or monitor patients' conditions.

Requirements for X Ray Tech Jobs in Virginia Beach

To become an X Ray technician in Virginia Beach, you will need to complete a formal education program and obtain certification from the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists (ARRT). Most X Ray tech jobs in Virginia Beach also require candidates to have a current state license.

How to Prepare for a Career as an X Ray Technician

If you are interested in pursuing a career as an X Ray technician in Virginia Beach, there are several steps you can take to prepare for this rewarding career:

Step 1: Research X Ray Tech Programs

Start by researching X Ray tech programs at accredited colleges or universities. Look for programs that offer hands-on experience with X Ray machines and other diagnostic imaging equipment.

Step 2: Complete an X Ray Tech Program

Once you have identified a program that meets your needs, enroll and complete your X Ray tech education. This typically takes two years for an associate degree or four years for a bachelor's degree.

Step 3: Obtain Certification from the ARRT

After completing your education, you will need to obtain certification from the ARRT. This involves passing a comprehensive exam that tests your knowledge and skills in X Ray technology.

Step 4: Apply for X Ray Tech Jobs in Virginia Beach

With your certification and state license in hand, you are now ready to start applying for X Ray tech jobs in Virginia Beach. Look for job postings on online job boards, healthcare provider websites, and other industry-specific job sites.

Opportunities for Advancement
