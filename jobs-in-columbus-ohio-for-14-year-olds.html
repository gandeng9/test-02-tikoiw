Jobs In Columbus Ohio For 14 Year Olds

Introduction

Columbus Ohio is a vibrant city with many opportunities for teenagers to explore. If you are 14 years old and looking for a job, there are plenty of options available to you. Whether you are looking for a part-time job after school or during the summer, this article will provide you with some ideas to get started.

Work Permits

Before you start your job search, it’s important to know that Ohio law requires minors under the age of 18 to obtain a work permit. You can get a work permit from your school or from the Ohio Department of Commerce. Your employer will also need to obtain a minor work permit endorsement from the Ohio Department of Commerce.

Summer Jobs

Summer is a great time to earn some extra money and gain work experience. Many businesses in Columbus Ohio offer summer jobs for teenagers. Some of the most popular options include working at a local pool, summer camp, or amusement park. You can also look for jobs at local businesses such as ice cream shops, restaurants, and retail stores.

Part-Time Jobs

If you are looking for a part-time job during the school year, there are plenty of options available. Many businesses in Columbus Ohio hire teenagers for part-time positions. Some examples include working at a grocery store, movie theater, or fast-food restaurant. You can also look for part-time jobs at local businesses such as pet stores, bookstores, and clothing stores.

Babysitting

Babysitting is a great way to earn money and gain experience working with children. Many families in Columbus Ohio are in need of babysitters, especially on weekends and during the summer. You can advertise your services online or through word of mouth. Make sure to get CPR certified and have references available.

Lawn Care

Another option for teenagers in Columbus Ohio is lawn care. Many homeowners in the area are in need of someone to mow their lawn, trim their bushes, and do other yard work. You can advertise your services online or through flyers in your neighborhood. Make sure to have a reliable lawn mower and other necessary tools.

Tutoring

If you excel in a particular subject, you can offer your services as a tutor. Many students in Columbus Ohio are in need of extra help in subjects such as math, science, and English. You can advertise your services online or through your school. Make sure to have references available.

Volunteering

Volunteering is a great way to gain work experience and give back to your community. Many organizations in Columbus Ohio are in need of volunteers, especially during the summer. Some examples include volunteering at a local animal shelter, food bank, or hospital. You can also look for volunteer opportunities through your school or church.

Conclusion

There are many options available for 14 year olds looking for jobs in Columbus Ohio. Whether you are looking for a summer job, part-time job, or volunteer opportunity, there is something for everyone. Make sure to obtain a work permit and have references available. Good luck with your job search!
