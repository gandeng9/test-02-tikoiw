Jobs Near Me Hiring At $20 An Hour – The Opportunities You Can't Miss In 2023

Introduction

Looking for a job is not easy, but it's even more challenging when you have a specific salary range in mind. However, if you're looking for jobs near you that pay $20 an hour or more, you're in luck! There are plenty of job opportunities available, and this article will give you an insight into the best ones.

Why $20 an Hour?

Twenty dollars an hour is a great hourly rate that can help you live a comfortable life. If you're working full-time, you can earn around $40,000 per year. Moreover, it's a great starting point for someone who's just starting their career or looking for a job change.

Jobs Near Me That Pay $20 an Hour

1. Customer Service Representative

Many companies are hiring customer service representatives who can work from home or in-person. As a customer service representative, you'll be responsible for answering customer inquiries, resolving complaints, and processing orders. You can earn around $20 an hour, depending on the company and your experience.

2. Delivery Driver

The demand for delivery drivers has increased significantly in recent years, and it's not going to slow down anytime soon. As a delivery driver, you'll be responsible for delivering packages, food, or other items to customers. You can earn $20 an hour, and some companies offer additional incentives such as tips and bonuses.

3. Data Entry Clerk

Data entry clerks are responsible for entering data into a computer system. It's a job that can be done remotely or in-person. You can earn around $20 an hour, depending on your experience and the company you're working for.

4. Medical Receptionist

Medical receptionists are responsible for greeting patients, scheduling appointments, and answering phone calls. You can work in a hospital, clinic, or private practice. The hourly rate can range from $15 to $25, depending on the location and your experience.

5. Property Manager

If you're interested in the real estate industry, becoming a property manager can be a great career choice. Property managers are responsible for managing properties, collecting rent, and maintaining the property. You can earn around $20 an hour, and some companies offer additional incentives such as bonuses and commissions.

Conclusion

Finding a job that pays $20 an hour or more can be challenging, but it's not impossible. The jobs mentioned in this article are just a few examples of the opportunities available. Keep searching, and you'll find the perfect job that suits your skills and experience. Good luck!
