Part Time Job At Kinokuniya Malaysia: A Great Opportunity For Students

Introduction

Are you a student looking for a part-time job that can help you earn some extra cash? If yes, then we have great news for you. Kinokuniya Malaysia is offering part-time job opportunities for students. Kinokuniya is a well-known bookstore chain that has a reputation for providing quality books and magazines to its customers. The company is now looking for reliable and hardworking individuals to join their team. Read on to find out more about this exciting opportunity.

Job Description

As a part-time employee at Kinokuniya, your main responsibility would be to assist customers with their purchases. You will also be required to maintain the cleanliness of the store and ensure that all books are properly displayed. Additionally, you may be asked to help with inventory management, which involves receiving and stocking new books.

Qualifications

To be eligible for this job, you must be at least 18 years old and currently enrolled in a tertiary institution. You should also have good communication skills and be able to work well in a team. Prior experience in customer service or retail is preferred but not required.

Working Hours

The working hours for part-time employees at Kinokuniya are flexible and can be adjusted to fit your schedule. However, you will be required to work at least 20 hours per week, which can be spread out over several days. This means that you can work part-time while still attending classes and studying for exams.

Benefits

Working at Kinokuniya has several benefits. Firstly, you will be able to earn some extra cash to support your studies. Secondly, you will gain valuable work experience that will look good on your resume. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to work in a fun and dynamic environment with like-minded individuals.

Application Process

If you are interested in applying for a part-time job at Kinokuniya, you can do so by visiting their website and filling out the online application form. You will be required to provide personal details, educational background, and work experience (if any). Once you have submitted your application, Kinokuniya will contact you if you are selected for an interview.

Conclusion

In conclusion, a part-time job at Kinokuniya Malaysia is a great opportunity for students who are looking for work that can fit around their studies. The job offers flexible working hours, valuable work experience, and the chance to work in a fun and dynamic environment. If you are interested in applying, visit Kinokuniya's website today and submit your application.
