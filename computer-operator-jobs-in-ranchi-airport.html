Introduction

Are you interested in a career in the aviation industry? Do you have a passion for technology and computers? Job Description

A computer operator in Ranchi airport is responsible for managing and maintaining computer systems, networks, and databases. They are also expected to troubleshoot technical issues and ensure the smooth functioning of all computer-related operations. Computer operators work in collaboration with other airport staff members to provide efficient and effective services to passengers and airport personnel.

Qualifications

To be eligible for a computer operator job in Ranchi airport, you must have a bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field. You should also have a strong knowledge of computer systems, programming languages, and database management. Additionally, you must have excellent communication skills and be able to work well under pressure.

Salary

The salary for a computer operator in Ranchi airport varies depending on the level of experience and qualifications. Typically, a computer operator can expect to earn between INR 20,000 to INR 35,000 per month. However, with additional certifications and experience, the salary can go up to INR 50,000 per month.

Skills Required

To excel in a computer operator job in Ranchi airport, you must possess the following skills:

Training and Certification

To enhance your skills and increase your chances of getting hired as a computer operator in Ranchi airport, you can consider obtaining additional training and certifications. Some of the popular certifications for computer operators include Certified Computer Operator (CCO), Certified Network Operator (CNO), and Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP).
