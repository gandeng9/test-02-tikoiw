Logistics Jobs In Chennai Airport

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

The Chennai International Airport, located in the south of India, is one of the busiest airports in the country. It serves as a hub for domestic and international flights, and the airport has a large logistics sector. The logistics jobs in Chennai airport have grown significantly over the years, and it has become a preferred destination for job seekers in the logistics industry.

Job Opportunities in Logistics

The logistics sector in Chennai airport provides job opportunities in various fields such as cargo handling, freight forwarding, warehouse management, customs clearance, and transportation. The cargo handling involves loading and unloading of cargo from aircraft, whereas the freight forwarding involves the transportation of goods from one place to another. Warehouse management is responsible for managing the storage and handling of goods, and customs clearance involves the clearance of goods from customs authorities. Transportation involves the movement of goods from one place to another.

Cargo Handling Jobs

The cargo handling jobs in Chennai airport are one of the most sought-after jobs in the logistics sector. The cargo handlers are responsible for loading and unloading of cargo from the aircraft. They are required to have a good knowledge of handling different types of cargo, including perishable goods, hazardous goods, and fragile items. They also need to have good physical stamina as the job involves a lot of lifting and carrying.

Freight Forwarding Jobs

The freight forwarding jobs in Chennai airport involve the transportation of goods from one place to another. The job requires the candidate to have good knowledge of the transportation industry, including the different modes of transportation such as air, sea, and land. They also need to have good communication skills to interact with clients and suppliers.

Warehouse Management Jobs

The warehouse management jobs in Chennai airport involve the management of storage and handling of goods. The warehouse manager is responsible for maintaining the inventory of goods, ensuring the safety of goods, and managing the movement of goods within the warehouse. They need to have good organizational skills, and knowledge of warehouse management software.

Customs Clearance Jobs

The customs clearance jobs in Chennai airport involve the clearance of goods from customs authorities. The customs clearance agent is responsible for ensuring that the goods comply with customs regulations, and they need to have good knowledge of the customs regulations and procedures.

Transportation Jobs

The transportation jobs in Chennai airport involve the movement of goods from one place to another. The transportation coordinator is responsible for coordinating the transportation of goods, including the mode of transportation and the route. They also need to have good knowledge of transportation regulations and procedures.

Qualifications Required

The logistics jobs in Chennai airport require candidates to have a minimum of a bachelor's degree in logistics, supply chain management, or a related field. Some of the jobs may also require candidates to have specific certifications, such as the International Air Transport Association (IATA) certification for cargo handling jobs.

Salary and Benefits

The salary for logistics jobs in Chennai airport varies depending on the job and the experience of the candidate. The average salary for cargo handlers is around INR 15,000-25,000 per month, whereas the salary for freight forwarders is around INR 25,000-40,000 per month. The salary for warehouse managers is around INR 30,000-50,000 per month, whereas the salary for customs clearance agents is around INR 20,000-35,000 per month. The transportation coordinators earn around INR 25,000-40,000 per month. The benefits of working in the logistics sector in Chennai airport include health insurance, retirement benefits, paid time off, and travel benefits.

Conclusion

The logistics sector in Chennai airport provides a wide range of job opportunities for job seekers in the logistics industry. The cargo handling, freight forwarding, warehouse management, customs clearance, and transportation jobs in Chennai airport offer exciting career opportunities with attractive salary packages and benefits. With the growing demand for logistics services, the job opportunities in Chennai airport are expected to increase in the coming years.
