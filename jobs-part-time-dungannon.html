Types of Part-Time Jobs in Dungannon

Dungannon has a diverse range of part-time job opportunities available. Some of the most popular part-time jobs in Dungannon include retail, hospitality, healthcare, customer service, and administration. There are also opportunities for part-time work in the education and training sector.

Retail

Retail is one of the most popular part-time job options in Dungannon. There are many retail stores located in the town centre, and they often require part-time staff to help with customer service and sales. Some of the most popular retail stores in Dungannon include Tesco, Asda, and Lidl.

Hospitality

The hospitality industry is another popular option for part-time work in Dungannon. There are many restaurants, cafes, and bars located in the town centre, and they often require part-time staff to help with food service and customer service. Some of the most popular hospitality venues in Dungannon include The Linen Green, The Argory, and The Hill of The O’Neill.

Healthcare

The healthcare sector is also a popular choice for part-time work in Dungannon. There are many healthcare providers located in the town, and they often require part-time staff to help with patient care and administrative tasks. Some of the most popular healthcare providers in Dungannon include South Tyrone Hospital and Dungannon Health Centre.

Customer Service

Many companies in Dungannon require part-time staff to help with customer service tasks. This includes answering phones, responding to emails, and helping customers with their queries. Some of the most popular companies in Dungannon that require part-time customer service staff include BT and Virgin Media.

Administration

The administration sector is also a popular choice for part-time work in Dungannon. Many companies require part-time staff to help with administrative tasks such as filing, answering phones, and organising appointments. Some of the most popular companies in Dungannon that require part-time administrative staff include Moy Park and First Trust Bank.

Tips for Finding Part-Time Jobs in Dungannon

Here are some tips to help you find the perfect part-time job in Dungannon:

1. Utilise Online Job Boards

Many companies in Dungannon advertise their part-time job vacancies on online job boards. Some of the most popular job boards include Indeed, Monster, and NIJobs. Make sure to regularly check these job boards for new part-time job vacancies in Dungannon.

Networking with local businesses is a great way to find part-time job opportunities in Dungannon. Attend local business events and connect with local business owners to enquire about any job vacancies that may be available.
