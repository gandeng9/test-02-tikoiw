Dinner Recipes With Ground Beef

Introduction

Are you tired of making the same old recipes with ground beef? Looking for some new and exciting dinner ideas? Look no further! We have compiled a list of delicious and easy dinner recipes that are sure to become your new favorites.

Tacos

Who doesn't love tacos? Brown your ground beef with your favorite taco seasoning and serve in tortillas with all your favorite toppings. Add some fresh cilantro and lime juice for an extra burst of flavor.

Spaghetti Bolognese

This classic Italian dish is perfect for a cozy night in. Brown your ground beef with onions and garlic, then simmer in a tomato sauce with herbs and spices. Serve over spaghetti and top with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Stuffed Peppers

Cut the tops off of bell peppers and remove the seeds. Brown your ground beef with rice, onions, and your favorite spices. Stuff the mixture into the peppers and bake in the oven until the peppers are tender.

Shepherd's Pie

This hearty British dish is perfect for a chilly evening. Brown your ground beef with onions, carrots, and peas. Top with creamy mashed potatoes and bake until golden brown.

Chili

Nothing beats a warm bowl of chili on a cold night. Brown your ground beef with onions, garlic, and chili powder. Add canned tomatoes, kidney beans, and your favorite spices. Let it simmer on the stove until the flavors meld together.

Meatloaf

This American classic is a crowd-pleaser. Mix ground beef with breadcrumbs, eggs, and your favorite spices. Form into a loaf and bake until golden brown. Serve with mashed potatoes and green beans for a comforting meal.

Burgers

Who doesn't love a juicy burger? Mix ground beef with Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Grill or cook in a cast-iron skillet until cooked through. Serve on buns with all your favorite toppings.

Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

This quick and easy dinner is perfect for busy weeknights. Brown your ground beef with garlic and ginger. Add broccoli florets and a sweet and savory sauce made with soy sauce, brown sugar, and rice vinegar. Serve over rice for a satisfying meal.

Beef Tamales

Making tamales from scratch may seem intimidating, but it's easier than you think. Make a filling with ground beef, corn, and spices. Spread the mixture onto masa dough and wrap in corn husks. Steam until cooked through and serve with salsa and guacamole.

Conclusion

With these delicious and easy dinner recipes, you'll never run out of ideas for using ground beef. From tacos to shepherd's pie, there's something for everyone. So, roll up your sleeves, get in the kitchen, and start cooking!
