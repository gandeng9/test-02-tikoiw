Introduction

Are you looking for a job in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool? With the increasing demand for online shopping, Matalan is expanding and looking for new employees to join their team. In this article, we will discuss the job opportunities available in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool and what you need to know before applying.

What is Matalan?

Matalan is a British fashion and homeware retailer, founded in 1985. It has over 200 stores across the UK and an online store, which is why they need a large warehouse to store their products. The Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool is one of their biggest warehouses in the UK, and they are always looking for new employees to join their team.

Job Opportunities Available

There are a variety of job opportunities available in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool. These include roles in packing, sorting, and dispatching products, as well as roles in the warehouse office, such as administration and management. They also offer apprenticeships for those who are just starting their career.

What are the Requirements?

To work in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool, you need to be over 18 years old and have the right to work in the UK. You also need to be physically fit, as the job involves a lot of walking, standing, and lifting heavy items. No previous experience is required, as full training will be provided.

What are the Working Hours?

The Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool operates 24/7, so there are a variety of shifts available. You may be required to work early mornings, evenings, or weekends. The hours can be long and may vary depending on the demand, but you will be paid overtime for any additional hours worked.

What is the Pay?

The pay for working in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool varies depending on the role and the hours worked. However, the minimum pay is above the national minimum wage, and you will also receive additional benefits such as staff discounts and a pension scheme.

How to Apply?

To apply for a job in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool, you can visit their website and search for available jobs. You can also visit their store in Liverpool and speak to a member of staff. Make sure you have an up-to-date CV and cover letter ready to send with your application.

Why Work for Matalan?

Working for Matalan has many benefits, including staff discounts, a pension scheme, and opportunities for career progression. They also offer a friendly and supportive working environment, with regular training and development opportunities.

Conclusion

If you are looking for a job in the Matalan Warehouse in Liverpool, there are many opportunities available. The job may involve physical work, but full training will be provided, and you will receive many benefits for working for Matalan. So, why not apply today and join their team?
