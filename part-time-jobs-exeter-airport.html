Part Time Jobs At Exeter Airport

Introduction

Exeter Airport is a popular regional airport located in Devon, England. The airport serves both domestic and international flights, making it a hub for travelers. If you're looking for a part-time job in the area, the airport might be a great place to start. In this article, we'll explore some of the part-time job opportunities available at Exeter Airport.

Customer Service

One of the most common part-time jobs at Exeter Airport is customer service. As a customer service representative, you'll be responsible for assisting travelers with their needs. This might include answering questions about flights, helping passengers find their way around the airport, or providing information on local attractions. Good communication skills and a friendly demeanor are a must for this type of job.

Security

Another part-time job opportunity at Exeter Airport is in security. As a security officer, you'll be responsible for ensuring the safety of passengers and staff. This might include checking bags and screening passengers for prohibited items. A background in security or law enforcement is preferred for this type of job.

Retail

Retail jobs are also available at Exeter Airport. As a retail associate, you'll be responsible for selling goods to travelers. This might include souvenirs, snacks, or duty-free items. Good customer service skills and a friendly demeanor are a must for this type of job.

Food Service

Food service jobs are another option at Exeter Airport. As a food service worker, you'll be responsible for preparing and serving food to travelers. This might include working in a restaurant or caf√©, or working at a food cart. Good customer service skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment are a must for this type of job.

Housekeeping

Housekeeping jobs are also available at Exeter Airport. As a housekeeper, you'll be responsible for keeping the airport clean and tidy. This might include sweeping floors, cleaning bathrooms, or emptying trash cans. Attention to detail and a strong work ethic are a must for this type of job.

Baggage Handler

Baggage handlers are responsible for loading and unloading luggage from planes. This is a physically demanding job that requires good communication skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. A background in logistics or transportation is preferred for this type of job.

Ground Crew

Ground crew jobs are also available at Exeter Airport. As a ground crew member, you'll be responsible for directing planes to their gates, loading and unloading cargo, and performing general maintenance on the airport grounds. Good communication skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment are a must for this type of job.

Conclusion

Whether you're looking for a customer service job, a security job, or a job in retail, food service, housekeeping, baggage handling, or ground crew, there are plenty of part-time job opportunities available at Exeter Airport. With good communication skills, a strong work ethic, and a friendly demeanor, you could be well on your way to a successful career in the aviation industry.
