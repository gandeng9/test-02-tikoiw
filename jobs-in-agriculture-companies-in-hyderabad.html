The Agriculture Industry in Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, has a thriving agriculture industry. The city's fertile land and favorable climate conditions make it an ideal location for the cultivation of crops such as rice, wheat, maize, and cotton. The agriculture sector in Hyderabad not only contributes to the state's economy but also provides livelihoods to many people.
