Introduction

Finding a job as a felon can be a daunting task, but it's not impossible. Colorado Springs is home to several businesses that are willing to give felons a second chance. In this article, we'll explore some of the jobs in Colorado Springs that hire felons.

Construction Jobs

Construction is one of the industries that hire felons in Colorado Springs. The construction industry is always looking for skilled laborers, and if you have experience in construction work, you may be able to land a job. Some of the jobs in construction that hire felons include carpenters, electricians, and plumbers.

Food Service Jobs

Another industry that's willing to hire felons is the food service industry. Many restaurants and fast-food chains in Colorado Springs are looking for employees who are dependable and hardworking. Some of the jobs in food service that hire felons include cooks, servers, and dishwashers.

Retail Jobs

Retail is another industry that's open to hiring felons. Many retail stores in Colorado Springs are looking for employees who are reliable and have excellent customer service skills. Some of the jobs in retail that hire felons include sales associates, cashiers, and stock clerks.

Trucking Jobs

If you have a commercial driver's license (CDL), you may be able to land a job in the trucking industry. Many trucking companies in Colorado Springs are willing to hire felons who have a CDL and a clean driving record. Some of the jobs in trucking that hire felons include delivery drivers and long-haul truckers.

Security Jobs

The security industry is another industry that's open to hiring felons. Many security companies in Colorado Springs are looking for employees who are trustworthy and dependable. Some of the jobs in security that hire felons include security guards, bouncers, and private investigators.

Healthcare Jobs

If you have experience in healthcare, you may be able to land a job in the healthcare industry. Many healthcare facilities in Colorado Springs are willing to hire felons who have the necessary training and experience. Some of the jobs in healthcare that hire felons include medical assistants, phlebotomists, and nursing assistants.

Manufacturing Jobs

Manufacturing is another industry that's open to hiring felons. Many manufacturing companies in Colorado Springs are looking for employees who are reliable and hardworking. Some of the jobs in manufacturing that hire felons include assembly line workers, machine operators, and maintenance technicians.

Conclusion

Finding a job as a felon can be challenging, but it's not impossible. There are several industries in Colorado Springs that are willing to give felons a second chance. Whether you have experience in construction, food service, retail, trucking, security, healthcare, or manufacturing, there's a job out there for you. Don't give up hope. Keep searching, and you'll eventually find the right job for you.
