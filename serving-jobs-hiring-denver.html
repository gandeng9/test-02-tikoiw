Serving Jobs Hiring In Denver: A Guide For Job Seekers In 2023

The Denver Service Industry in 2023

Denver's service industry has grown rapidly in the past decade, with more and more restaurants, bars, and cafes opening up each year. As a result, there are plenty of opportunities for job seekers looking for serving jobs in the area. Whether you're a seasoned hospitality professional or just starting out in the industry, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to find your next job in Denver.

Types of Serving Jobs Available in Denver

There are many different types of serving jobs available in Denver, ranging from fine dining to casual fast food. Some of the most common roles include server, bartender, host/hostess, and barista. Depending on your experience and qualifications, you may be able to find a job in a high-end restaurant or a more relaxed cafe.

Qualifications and Skills Needed for Serving Jobs in Denver

While some serving jobs in Denver may not require any prior experience, many employers will prefer candidates who have worked in the industry before. Additionally, certain roles such as bartending may require specific certifications or qualifications. In terms of skills, a friendly and outgoing personality, good communication, and the ability to handle pressure are all essential for success in the service industry.

How to Find Serving Jobs in Denver

Online Job Boards

One of the easiest ways to find serving jobs in Denver is to search online job boards. Websites such as Indeed, Monster, and Glassdoor all have dedicated sections for hospitality jobs in the area. You can also check out job boards specifically for the service industry, such as Proven and Hcareers.

Networking and Referrals

Another effective way to find serving jobs in Denver is to network with industry professionals and ask for referrals. Attend local industry events, join professional organizations, and connect with other servers and bartenders on social media. If you have friends or acquaintances who work in the industry, ask if they know of any job openings or can refer you to their employer.

Directly Contacting Employers

Finally, you can also try directly contacting employers to inquire about job openings. Check out the websites of your favorite restaurants, cafes, and bars, and look for a "careers" or "jobs" section. You can also visit these establishments in person and ask if they are hiring. Make sure to bring a copy of your resume and dress professionally for any in-person interactions.

Top Serving Jobs Hiring in Denver in 2023

1. Server at The Kitchen

The Kitchen is a popular farm-to-table restaurant located in downtown Denver. They are currently hiring servers with at least two years of experience in a fine dining setting. Candidates should have excellent communication skills, a passion for food and wine, and a friendly and professional demeanor.

2. Bartender at The Way Back

The Way Back is a trendy bar in the RiNo neighborhood of Denver. They are looking for an experienced bartender to join their team, with a focus on craft cocktails and a deep knowledge of spirits. Candidates should have at least three years of bartending experience and be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment.

3. Host/Hostess at Snooze

Snooze is a popular breakfast spot with several locations throughout Denver. They are currently hiring host/hostesses to greet guests, manage waitlists, and ensure a positive guest experience. Candidates should have excellent customer service skills and be able to multitask in a busy restaurant environment.

Tips for Nailing Your Serving Job Interview

1. Dress Professionally

Make sure to dress professionally for your interview, even if the restaurant has a more casual dress code. This shows that you take the job seriously and are committed to making a good impression.

2. Research the Restaurant

Do your research on the restaurant before your interview. Look up their menu, read reviews, and familiarize yourself with their style and atmosphere. This will show that you are genuinely interested in the job and have done your homework.

3. Be Confident and Friendly

During the interview, make sure to be confident and friendly. Smile, make eye contact, and engage with the interviewer. Show that you have a positive attitude and are excited about the opportunity to work for their establishment.

Conclusion

Denver's service industry is thriving, and there are plenty of opportunities for job seekers looking for serving jobs in the area. Whether you're just starting out in the industry or have years of experience, following these tips and guidelines will help you find your next job in Denver and nail your interview.
