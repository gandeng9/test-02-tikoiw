Jobs In Trivandrum Airport For 12Th Pass

Introduction

Trivandrum International Airport is one of the major airports in South India. It is the gateway to Kerala, the God's own country. The airport is situated in the capital city of Thiruvananthapuram, which is also known as Trivandrum. The airport is managed by the Airports Authority of India (AAI). The airport offers various job opportunities for 12th pass candidates.

Jobs in Trivandrum Airport

1. Security Personnel

Trivandrum Airport requires security personnel to ensure the safety and security of passengers and airport property. The minimum qualification required is 12th pass. The job involves checking passengers and their luggage, maintaining security standards, and reporting any suspicious activities.

2. Customer Service Executive

The airport requires customer service executives to assist passengers and provide them with necessary information. The minimum qualification required is 12th pass. The job involves handling passenger queries, providing assistance, and ensuring customer satisfaction.

3. Cargo Handler

The airport requires cargo handlers to load and unload cargo from aircraft. The minimum qualification required is 12th pass. The job involves handling cargo, ensuring timely delivery, and maintaining safety standards.

4. Ground Staff

The airport requires ground staff to assist in various activities like check-in, boarding, and baggage handling. The minimum qualification required is 12th pass. The job involves assisting passengers, ensuring timely departure, and maintaining safety standards.

How to Apply?

The interested candidates can apply for the jobs in Trivandrum Airport through the official website of Airports Authority of India (AAI). The website provides all the necessary information regarding the job vacancies and the application process.

Conclusion

Trivandrum Airport provides various job opportunities for 12th pass candidates. The jobs offer good pay, benefits, and job security. The interested candidates can apply for the jobs through the official website of AAI. The airport is a great place to start a career in the aviation industry.
