Jobs Hiring 16 Year Olds In Columbus Ohio In 2023

Introduction

Are you a 16-year-old looking for a job in Columbus, Ohio? You're not alone. Many teens want to earn some extra cash during their summer break, or even during the school year. However, finding a job as a teenager can be challenging. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore some of the best job opportunities available for 16-year-olds in Columbus, Ohio in 2023.

Summer Jobs

Summer is the perfect time for teenagers to make some extra money. Many businesses in Columbus, Ohio hire teenagers during the summer months. Some of the most popular summer jobs for 16-year-olds include working as a lifeguard at a pool or water park, working at an amusement park, or working as a camp counselor. These jobs not only pay well, but they also give you the opportunity to meet new people and make new friends.

Lifeguard Jobs

If you're a strong swimmer, a job as a lifeguard might be perfect for you. Many community pools and water parks in Columbus, Ohio hire teenagers as lifeguards during the summer months. As a lifeguard, your job is to ensure the safety of swimmers and enforce pool rules. You'll need to have a current lifeguard certification and be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment.

Amusement Park Jobs

Another popular summer job for 16-year-olds in Columbus, Ohio is working at an amusement park. Amusement parks like Cedar Point and Kings Island hire thousands of teenagers each year to work in various positions, including ride operators, food service, and retail. These jobs often come with perks like free admission to the park and discounts on food and merchandise.

Camp Counselor Jobs

If you love working with kids, a job as a camp counselor might be perfect for you. Many summer camps in Columbus, Ohio hire teenagers as counselors. As a counselor, your job is to ensure the safety and well-being of campers while also leading fun activities and games. This job is perfect for teens who enjoy being outdoors and working in a team environment.

Part-Time Jobs

If you're looking for a job during the school year, there are still plenty of opportunities available. Many businesses in Columbus, Ohio hire teenagers for part-time positions. Some of the most popular part-time jobs for 16-year-olds include working at a fast-food restaurant, retail store, or movie theater.

Fast-Food Jobs

Fast-food restaurants like McDonald's and Taco Bell often hire teenagers for part-time positions. As a fast-food worker, your job is to take orders, prepare food, and keep the restaurant clean. This job is perfect for teens who are looking for flexible hours and a fast-paced work environment.

Retail Jobs

Retail stores like Target and Walmart also hire teenagers for part-time positions. As a retail worker, your job is to assist customers with their needs, stock shelves, and keep the store clean. This job is perfect for teens who enjoy working with people and have good communication skills.

Movie Theater Jobs

Movie theaters like AMC and Regal often hire teenagers for part-time positions. As a movie theater worker, your job is to sell tickets, serve concessions, and clean theaters between showings. This job is perfect for teens who enjoy movies and want to work in a fun environment.

Conclusion

Whether you're looking for a summer job or a part-time job during the school year, there are plenty of opportunities available for 16-year-olds in Columbus, Ohio. From working as a lifeguard at a pool to serving concessions at a movie theater, there's a job out there for everyone. So get out there and start applying!
