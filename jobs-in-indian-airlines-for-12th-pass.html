Jobs In Indian Airlines For 12Th Pass: Your Ultimate Guide

Introduction

If you're a 12th pass student looking for a job in the aviation industry, then you've come to the right place. The Indian aviation sector has been growing at an impressive rate, creating numerous job opportunities for young and dynamic candidates like you. In this article, we'll be discussing the various jobs available in the Indian airlines for 12th pass candidates and what it takes to land one of these jobs.

Types of Jobs Available

There are several types of jobs available in the Indian airlines for 12th pass candidates. Some of the most popular ones include: - Cabin Crew - Ground Staff - Customer Service Executive - Security Staff - Cargo Executive - Ticketing Executive

Cabin Crew

Cabin Crew members are responsible for ensuring the safety and comfort of passengers during a flight. They are also responsible for serving food and beverages and providing first aid in case of an emergency. To become a cabin crew member, you need to have excellent communication skills, a pleasing personality, and the ability to work in a team.

Ground Staff

Ground staff members are responsible for handling the administrative and operational tasks at the airport. They are responsible for checking in passengers, handling baggage, and coordinating with other departments to ensure smooth operations. To become a ground staff member, you need to have good communication skills, basic computer knowledge, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Customer Service Executive

Customer service executives are responsible for handling customer queries and complaints. They are also responsible for providing information about flights and other services offered by the airline. To become a customer service executive, you need to have excellent communication skills, good problem-solving skills, and the ability to work under pressure.

Security Staff

Security staff members are responsible for ensuring the safety and security of passengers and airport staff. They are responsible for screening passengers and their baggage, monitoring the airport premises, and responding to emergencies. To become a security staff member, you need to have good physical fitness, sharp observation skills, and the ability to work in a team.

Cargo Executive

Cargo executives are responsible for handling the cargo operations of the airline. They are responsible for loading and unloading cargo, maintaining cargo records, and coordinating with other departments for smooth operations. To become a cargo executive, you need to have basic computer knowledge, good communication skills, and the ability to work in a team.

Ticketing Executive

Ticketing executives are responsible for selling and booking tickets for passengers. They are also responsible for providing information about flights and other services offered by the airline. To become a ticketing executive, you need to have good communication skills, basic computer knowledge, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Qualifications Required

To be eligible for jobs in Indian airlines for 12th pass candidates, you need to meet certain qualifications. Some of the qualifications required for different types of jobs are: - Cabin Crew: 12th pass with a minimum height of 157 cm for females and 170 cm for males - Ground Staff: 12th pass with basic computer knowledge - Customer Service Executive: 12th pass with good communication skills - Security Staff: 12th pass with good physical fitness - Cargo Executive: 12th pass with basic computer knowledge - Ticketing Executive: 12th pass with basic computer knowledge

How to Apply

To apply for jobs in Indian airlines for 12th pass candidates, you can visit the official website of the airline or check for job openings in leading job portals. You need to fill in the application form and upload your resume along with a passport-sized photograph. You may also be required to pay an application fee.

Conclusion

Jobs in Indian airlines for 12th pass candidates can be a great career opportunity for those who are passionate about the aviation industry. With the right qualifications and skills, you can land a job in one of the leading airlines in India. So, start preparing yourself and take the first step towards a rewarding career in the aviation industry.
