Introduction

Are you looking for part-time jobs near you? Do you want to find the best opportunities on Gumtree? In this article, we will provide you with tips, news, and reviews about the most popular part-time jobs available in 2023. Whether you want to work as a freelancer, a delivery driver, or a tutor, Gumtree has plenty of options for you to choose from. Let's explore the possibilities!

News

1. Gumtree Launches New Job Board for Part-Time Workers

Gumtree has recently launched a new job board specifically designed for part-time workers. This new feature allows job seekers to filter their search by hours per week, hourly rate, and location, making it easier than ever to find the perfect job near you. With thousands of new listings added every day, Gumtree's job board is quickly becoming the go-to destination for part-time work.

2. Part-Time Remote Work on the Rise

In 2023, more and more companies are offering part-time remote work options. This means that you can work from home or anywhere else in the world while still earning a steady income. Whether you are a writer, a designer, or a customer service representative, there are plenty of remote part-time jobs available on Gumtree.

Tips

1. Customize Your Resume and Cover Letter

When applying for part-time jobs on Gumtree, it's important to customize your resume and cover letter to fit the job requirements. Make sure to highlight your relevant skills and experience, and explain why you are the best candidate for the job.

2. Be Flexible with Your Schedule

Many part-time jobs on Gumtree require flexibility with your schedule. Make sure to communicate your availability to your employer and be willing to work evenings, weekends, or holidays if needed.

Reviews

1. Deliveroo Rider Review

If you enjoy cycling and want to earn money while staying active, becoming a Deliveroo rider might be the perfect job for you. According to reviews on Gumtree, Deliveroo riders enjoy flexible schedules, competitive pay, and a supportive community of fellow riders.

2. Tutor Review

If you have expertise in a particular subject, you can become a tutor on Gumtree and help students achieve their academic goals. According to reviews, tutors on Gumtree enjoy the flexibility of setting their own rates and schedules, as well as the satisfaction of helping others succeed.

Tutorial

How to Create a Winning Gumtree Profile

If you want to increase your chances of finding the perfect part-time job on Gumtree, it's important to create a winning profile. Here are some tips:

  1. Include a professional profile picture
  2. Highlight your relevant skills and experience
  3. Provide details about your availability and desired hourly rate
  4. Respond promptly to messages from potential employers

Conclusion

Part-time jobs on Gumtree are a great way to earn extra income, gain new skills, and meet new people. Whether you are a student, a stay-at-home parent, or someone who wants to supplement their full-time income, Gumtree has plenty of options for you to choose from. By following our tips, staying up-to-date with the latest news, reading reviews, and creating a winning profile, you can find the best part-time job near you in 2023.
