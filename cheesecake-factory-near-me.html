The Cheesecake Factory History and Fame

The Cheesecake Factory is a well-known American restaurant chain that specializes in cheesecakes, desserts, and a wide variety of dishes. Founded in 1972, The Cheesecake Factory has grown to over 200 locations in the United States and other countries. The chain has gained massive popularity for its delicious cheesecakes that come in over 30 different flavors.
