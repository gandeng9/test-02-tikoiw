Back Office Jobs In Amazon Bangalore: A Comprehensive Guide

Introduction

Amazon is one of the largest e-commerce companies in the world, and it has a significant presence in Bangalore, India. The company offers a wide range of job opportunities, including back office jobs. In this article, we will explore the various types of back office jobs available in Amazon Bangalore, their roles and responsibilities, and the skills required to excel in these roles.

What are Back Office Jobs?

Back office jobs refer to the non-customer-facing roles in a company. These jobs are essential for the smooth functioning of the organization. The back office team is responsible for handling various administrative and support functions, such as data entry, record keeping, inventory management, and customer service.

Types of Back Office Jobs in Amazon Bangalore

Amazon Bangalore offers a wide range of back office jobs, including:

1. Data Entry Operator

Data entry operators are responsible for entering data into the company's database. They must have excellent typing speed, accuracy, and attention to detail.

2. Inventory Control Specialist

Inventory control specialists are responsible for managing the company's inventory, ensuring that the stock levels are maintained, and the products are delivered on time.

3. Customer Service Associate

Customer service associates are responsible for handling customer queries, resolving complaints, and providing assistance to customers.

4. Accounts Payable Processor

Accounts payable processors are responsible for managing the company's accounts payable, ensuring that the bills are paid on time, and the records are updated.

5. HR Coordinator

HR coordinators are responsible for supporting the HR team in various administrative tasks, including recruitment, onboarding, and employee engagement.

Role and Responsibilities

The roles and responsibilities of back office jobs in Amazon Bangalore vary depending on the position. However, some common responsibilities include: - Handling administrative tasks such as data entry, record keeping, and inventory management. - Providing support to other departments such as customer service, HR, and finance. - Ensuring compliance with company policies and procedures. - Maintaining accurate and up-to-date records.

Skills Required

The skills required for back office jobs in Amazon Bangalore vary depending on the position. However, some common skills include: - Excellent communication skills. - Good computer skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Office and other software applications. - Attention to detail and accuracy. - Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines. - Good time management skills.

Conclusion

Back office jobs are an essential part of any organization, and Amazon Bangalore offers a wide range of such jobs. These jobs offer a great career opportunity for individuals who are looking for stable and challenging roles. If you are interested in back office jobs in Amazon Bangalore, make sure to check out the company's website for current openings.
