Jobs In Delhi Airport For 12Th Pass Freshers

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Delhi Airport is one of the busiest and largest airports in India. It provides numerous employment opportunities for candidates who have completed their 12th standard. In this article, we will discuss the various job opportunities available for 12th pass freshers in Delhi Airport.

1. Security Personnel

Security is a crucial aspect of any airport. As a 12th pass fresher, you can apply for the position of security personnel. The job involves maintaining the safety and security of the airport premises and its passengers. You will be responsible for screening passengers, baggage, and cargo to ensure that no prohibited items are being carried.

Additionally, you will be required to coordinate with other airport personnel in case of any emergency situations. The job requires excellent communication skills, physical fitness, and the ability to handle stressful situations.

2. Customer Service Executive

As a customer service executive, you will be responsible for assisting passengers with their queries and concerns. You will be required to provide information about flight schedules, baggage handling, and other airport-related services. Additionally, you may also be required to handle passenger complaints and resolve them in a professional manner.

The job requires excellent communication skills, a pleasant personality, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. You should also be proficient in handling computer systems and have a basic understanding of the aviation industry.

3. Ground Staff

Ground staff plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth functioning of the airport. As a ground staff member, your job will involve performing various tasks like baggage handling, cargo loading/unloading, and aircraft cleaning. You will also be required to assist passengers with boarding and disembarking from the aircraft.

The job requires physical fitness, the ability to work in a team, and excellent communication skills. You should also be comfortable working in shifts and have a basic understanding of the aviation industry.

4. Cargo Handler

Cargo handling is an essential function of any airport. As a cargo handler, you will be responsible for loading and unloading cargo from the aircraft. You will also be required to ensure that the cargo is properly packed and labeled.

The job requires physical fitness, the ability to work in a team, and excellent communication skills. You should also be comfortable working in shifts and have a basic understanding of cargo handling procedures.

5. Ramp Agent

As a ramp agent, you will be responsible for directing aircraft to their parking spots, loading and unloading baggage, and ensuring that the aircraft is fueled and ready for departure. You will also be required to perform various other tasks related to aircraft handling.

The job requires physical fitness, the ability to work in a team, and excellent communication skills. You should also have a basic understanding of aircraft handling procedures and be comfortable working in shifts.

Conclusion

Delhi Airport provides numerous employment opportunities for 12th pass freshers. The jobs mentioned in this article are just a few examples of the various positions available. To apply for any of these jobs, you will need to visit the Delhi Airport website and check for job vacancies. Make sure that you meet the eligibility criteria before applying. Good luck with your job search!
