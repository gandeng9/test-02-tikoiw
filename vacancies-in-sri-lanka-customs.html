Vacancies In Sri Lanka Customs: A Comprehensive Guide

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Sri Lanka Customs is a government organization responsible for regulating and facilitating international trade. It is one of the oldest government departments in the country, established in 1806. With a workforce of over 4,000 employees, Sri Lanka Customs plays a vital role in maintaining the country's economic stability.

Available Vacancies

Sri Lanka Customs regularly announces job vacancies for various positions. These positions include customs officers, assistant customs officers, clerks, and technical officers. The vacancies are advertised in leading newspapers and on the official website of Sri Lanka Customs.

Customs Officer

A customs officer is responsible for enforcing customs laws and regulations. The position requires a degree in any field from a recognized university. Applicants must also pass a competitive exam and undergo a training program.

Assistant Customs Officer

An assistant customs officer assists customs officers in their duties. The position requires a diploma or degree in any field from a recognized institution. Applicants must also pass a competitive exam and undergo a training program.

Clerk

A clerk is responsible for maintaining records and providing administrative support. The position requires a diploma or degree in any field from a recognized institution.

Technical Officer

A technical officer is responsible for maintaining and repairing customs equipment. The position requires a diploma or degree in mechanical or electrical engineering from a recognized institution.

Application Process

To apply for a position in Sri Lanka Customs, applicants must submit an application form along with the required documents. The application form can be downloaded from the official website of Sri Lanka Customs. The required documents include a copy of the birth certificate, educational certificates, and national identity card.

Selection Process

The selection process for vacancies in Sri Lanka Customs includes a written exam, an interview, and a medical examination. The written exam tests the applicant's knowledge of customs laws and regulations. The interview assesses the applicant's communication skills and suitability for the position. The medical examination ensures that the applicant is physically fit for the job.

Salary and Benefits

The salary and benefits for positions in Sri Lanka Customs vary depending on the position and experience. Customs officers and assistant customs officers receive a basic salary along with allowances for housing, transport, and medical expenses. Clerks and technical officers receive a basic salary along with allowances for housing and transport.

Conclusion

Sri Lanka Customs offers a challenging and rewarding career for those interested in international trade and customs regulations. With a competitive salary and benefits package, it is an attractive option for job seekers in Sri Lanka. Keep an eye out for vacancies and apply to start your career in Sri Lanka Customs.
