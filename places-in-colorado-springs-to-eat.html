Best Places To Eat In Colorado Springs In 2023

Introduction

Colorado Springs is a beautiful city located in Colorado, USA. It is known for its stunning scenery, outdoor recreation, and delicious food. In this article, we will explore the top places to eat in Colorado Springs in 2023. Whether you're a local or a tourist, these restaurants are sure to satisfy your taste buds.

The Rabbit Hole

The Rabbit Hole is a unique restaurant located in downtown Colorado Springs. It offers a creative menu that features locally sourced ingredients and a variety of craft cocktails. The restaurant's ambiance is whimsical and fun, with a decor that resembles Alice in Wonderland. Must-try dishes include the Rabbit Hole Burger, the Mac and Cheese Bites, and the Mushroom Risotto.

Four by Brother Luck

Four by Brother Luck is an upscale restaurant that focuses on seasonal and locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant is owned by Chef Brother Luck, who was a finalist on Bravo's Top Chef. The menu changes frequently, but some of the popular dishes include the Elk Tartare, the Bison Short Ribs, and the Sweet Potato Gnocchi.

Pizzeria Rustica

For those who love pizza, Pizzeria Rustica is a must-visit. The restaurant offers wood-fired pizzas that are made with fresh ingredients and cooked to perfection. The menu also features a variety of salads, appetizers, and desserts. The restaurant has a cozy ambiance, with outdoor seating available during the warmer months.

Odyssey Gastropub

Odyssey Gastropub is a casual restaurant that offers a variety of dishes from around the world. The restaurant's menu changes frequently, but some of the popular dishes include the Korean Fried Chicken, the Fish and Chips, and the Beef Stroganoff. The restaurant also has a great selection of craft beers and cocktails.

Streetcar520

Streetcar520 is a modern American restaurant that offers a variety of dishes made with locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant has a trendy ambiance, with a large bar and outdoor seating available. Some of the popular dishes include the Grilled Colorado Lamb Chops, the Pan-Seared Scallops, and the Streetcar Burger.

The Wobbly Olive

The Wobbly Olive is a cocktail bar and restaurant that offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks. The restaurant's menu changes frequently, but some of the popular dishes include the Shrimp and Grits, the Pork Belly Ramen, and the Charcuterie Board. The bar also has a great selection of craft cocktails and local beers.

Conclusion

Colorado Springs is a foodie's paradise. With so many great restaurants to choose from, it can be hard to decide where to eat. The restaurants listed in this article are sure to satisfy your cravings and provide a memorable dining experience. Whether you're looking for upscale dining or a casual night out, Colorado Springs has something for everyone.
