Introduction

India is a country with a rich heritage of education and learning. With a growing number of professionals seeking higher education while working, part-time PhD programs have become popular in recent years. These programs offer flexibility and convenience for working professionals who wish to further their education without leaving their jobs. In this article, we will explore the best part-time PhD programs in India.

1. Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi

IIT Delhi is one of the most prestigious institutes in India and offers part-time PhD programs in various fields. The duration of the program is typically 4-6 years and classes are held on weekends to accommodate working professionals. The institute has a strong network of research collaborations with industry and academia, providing ample opportunities for research.

2. Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune

IISER Pune is another top institute that offers part-time PhD programs in various fields. The program duration is typically 5-6 years and classes are held on weekends. The institute has state-of-the-art research facilities and a strong focus on interdisciplinary research.

The institute has a strong focus on research and provides ample opportunities for students to engage in interdisciplinary research.

The program is designed to cater to the needs of working professionals and the duration is typically 4-6 years.
