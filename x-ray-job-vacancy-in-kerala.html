X Ray Job Vacancy In Kerala – A Comprehensive Guide

Introduction

The healthcare industry is booming in Kerala, and with it comes an increasing demand for skilled professionals. One such profession that is in high demand is that of X-ray technicians. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about X-ray job vacancies in Kerala and how to land your dream job.

What is an X-ray Technician?

An X-ray technician, also known as a radiologic technologist, is a healthcare professional who operates imaging equipment to create images of the human body. These images are used by doctors to diagnose and treat medical conditions. X-ray technicians must be knowledgeable about radiation safety and must follow strict procedures to ensure the safety of themselves and their patients.

Job Outlook in Kerala

The demand for X-ray technicians in Kerala is on the rise. With the increasing number of hospitals and clinics in the state, there is a need for skilled professionals who can operate imaging equipment. The job outlook for X-ray technicians in Kerala is promising, and there are plenty of job opportunities available.

Qualifications Required

To become an X-ray technician in Kerala, you must have a diploma or degree in radiography. The course duration is usually 2-3 years. You must also be registered with the Kerala Paramedical Council. In addition to formal qualifications, X-ray technicians must have excellent communication skills, be able to work under pressure, and have a keen eye for detail.

Job Responsibilities

The job responsibilities of an X-ray technician include preparing patients for imaging procedures, positioning the patient and the equipment, adjusting the equipment to obtain the best images, and ensuring the safety of the patient and themselves. They must also maintain and troubleshoot imaging equipment.

Salary and Benefits

X-ray technicians in Kerala can expect to earn a starting salary of around Rs. 20,000-25,000 per month. With experience and further qualifications, the salary can go up to Rs. 50,000-60,000 per month. In addition to a good salary, X-ray technicians in Kerala also enjoy benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and retirement plans.

How to Find X-ray Job Vacancies in Kerala?

There are many ways to find X-ray job vacancies in Kerala. You can search online job portals such as Naukri and Monster, or you can visit hospital and clinic websites to see if they have any job openings. Networking is also an effective way to find job vacancies. You can attend job fairs and conferences and connect with professionals in the industry.

Preparing for the Interview

Once you have applied for an X-ray job vacancy in Kerala, it is important to prepare for the interview. Research the hospital or clinic where you have applied, and familiarize yourself with their values and mission. Practice answering common interview questions, such as why you want to work as an X-ray technician and what your strengths are.

Conclusion

X-ray job vacancies in Kerala are on the rise, and there are plenty of opportunities available for skilled professionals. To become an X-ray technician, you must have the required qualifications and skills, and be registered with the Kerala Paramedical Council. With a good salary and benefits, this is a promising career option for those interested in healthcare.
