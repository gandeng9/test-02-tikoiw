Introduction

Finding a job can be challenging, especially for those with a criminal record. However, there are employers in Tucson who are willing to hire people with a felony conviction. In this article, we will discuss some of the jobs in Tucson that hire felons.

1. Construction

Construction is a field that is open to felons. Many construction companies are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have experience in the industry. Jobs in this field include carpenters, electricians, and plumbers.

2. Food Service

Food service is another industry that is open to felons. Many restaurants and fast-food chains are willing to hire people with a criminal record. Jobs in this field include cooks, servers, and dishwashers.

3. Landscaping

Landscaping is a field that is open to felons. Many landscaping companies are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have experience in the industry. Jobs in this field include lawn care technicians, tree trimmers, and landscape designers.

4. Retail

Retail is another industry that is open to felons. Many retail stores are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have customer service experience. Jobs in this field include sales associates, cashiers, and stock clerks.

5. Transportation

Transportation is a field that is open to felons. Many transportation companies are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have a commercial driver's license. Jobs in this field include truck drivers, delivery drivers, and bus drivers.

6. Manufacturing

Manufacturing is another industry that is open to felons. Many manufacturing companies are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have experience in the industry. Jobs in this field include machine operators, assemblers, and quality control inspectors.

7. Healthcare

Healthcare is a field that is open to felons. Many healthcare facilities are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have medical training or experience. Jobs in this field include medical assistants, phlebotomists, and nursing assistants.

8. Janitorial Services

Janitorial services is another industry that is open to felons. Many cleaning companies are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have experience in the industry. Jobs in this field include janitors, custodians, and housekeepers.

9. Call Centers

Call centers are a field that is open to felons. Many call centers are willing to hire people with a criminal record, especially if they have customer service experience. Jobs in this field include customer service representatives, sales agents, and technical support specialists.

10. Freelancing

Freelancing is another option for felons who are having a hard time finding a job. Freelancing allows you to work from home and set your own schedule. Jobs in this field include writing, graphic design, and web development.

Conclusion

Finding a job as a felon can be challenging, but it's not impossible. There are many industries in Tucson that are open to felons, including construction, food service, landscaping, retail, transportation, manufacturing, healthcare, janitorial services, call centers, and freelancing. With persistence and determination, felons can find a job and start a new chapter in their lives.
