Jobs In Queens For 15 Year Olds: Your Ultimate Guide

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Being a 15-year-old can be tough when it comes to finding a job. You may be too young for most positions, but you still need to earn some extra cash. If you live in Queens, New York, then you’re in luck. Here’s a guide to help you find jobs in Queens for 15-year-olds.

Types of Jobs

Retail Positions

Many stores in Queens hire 15-year-olds to work as cashiers, stockers, or greeters. You can apply to places like Target, Walmart, and Best Buy. You may also want to check out local shops and boutiques.

Food Service

Restaurants, cafes, and fast-food chains are always in need of employees. You can work as a server, busser, or dishwasher. You can also apply to local bakeries or ice cream parlors.

Entertainment Industry

If you’re interested in the entertainment industry, you can work at a movie theater, theme park, or museum. You may also find opportunities to work as a performer or intern.

How to Get Hired

Get a Work Permit

Before you can start working, you need to get a work permit. You can get one from your school’s guidance counselor or online.

Prepare a Resume

Even if you don’t have any work experience, you can still create a resume to showcase your skills and interests. Include your education, extracurricular activities, and any volunteer work.

Apply Online or In Person

Many companies have online applications, so you can apply from the comfort of your own home. However, you may want to consider applying in person to make a good impression.

Working as a 15-Year-Old

Work Hours and Restrictions

As a 15-year-old, you’re limited in the number of hours you can work and the times you can work. You can work a maximum of 18 hours per week during the school year and 40 hours per week during the summer. You also can’t work before 7am or after 7pm.

Pay and Benefits

The minimum wage in New York is $15 per hour, so you can expect to earn at least that much. Some positions may offer benefits like free meals or discounts on merchandise.

Conclusion

There are plenty of jobs in Queens for 15-year-olds. Whether you’re interested in retail, food service, or the entertainment industry, there’s something for everyone. Just remember to get a work permit, prepare a resume, and apply online or in person. Good luck!
