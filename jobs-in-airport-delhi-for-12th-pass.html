Jobs In Airport Delhi For 12Th Pass: A Comprehensive Guide

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

If you are a 12th pass candidate and looking for job opportunities in the aviation industry, then Delhi airport can be a great option for you. The aviation sector in India is growing at a rapid pace, creating numerous job opportunities for aspiring candidates. In this article, we will discuss the different job roles available in Delhi airport for 12th pass candidates.

Job Roles

1. Ground Staff

Ground staff is responsible for handling various tasks on the ground, such as check-ins, baggage handling, and security checks. The job requires good communication skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

2. Customer Service

As a customer service executive, your job will be to assist passengers with their queries, provide them with relevant information, and ensure their overall satisfaction. Good communication skills and a friendly demeanor are essential for this job.

3. Security Staff

The security staff is responsible for ensuring the safety and security of passengers and airport property. The job requires physical fitness and the ability to work in shifts.

4. Cargo Staff

The cargo staff is responsible for handling and transporting cargo to and from the airport. The job requires physical fitness and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for these jobs, you need to have completed your 12th standard from a recognized board. You should also possess good communication skills, a friendly demeanor, and the ability to work in a team.

Salary and Benefits

The salary for these jobs ranges from Rs. 15,000 to Rs. 25,000 depending on the job role and experience. Apart from the salary, you will also receive various benefits such as medical insurance, travel allowance, and retirement benefits.

How to Apply

To apply for these jobs, you can visit the official website of Delhi airport or contact the airport authorities directly. You will need to submit your resume and attend an interview to get selected for the job.

Tips for Success

To be successful in these jobs, you should possess good communication skills, a friendly demeanor, and the ability to work in a team. You should also be physically fit and willing to work in shifts.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Delhi airport offers numerous job opportunities for 12th pass candidates in various job roles such as ground staff, customer service, security staff, and cargo staff. To be eligible for these jobs, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills and a friendly demeanor. With the right skills and attitude, you can have a successful career in the aviation industry.
