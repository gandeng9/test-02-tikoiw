12Th Pass Job In Ranchi Airport: Opportunities And Requirements In 2023

Introduction

Are you a 12th pass student looking for a career opportunity in the aviation industry? Ranchi Airport offers several job opportunities for candidates who have completed their 12th grade.

Requirements

To apply for a job in Ranchi Airport, candidates must meet certain requirements. The candidate should have completed their 12th grade with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized board. Additionally, they must be proficient in English and Hindi and possess excellent communication skills. Candidates must also be physically fit and able to work in shifts.

Job Opportunities

Ranchi Airport offers a range of job opportunities for 12th pass candidates in various fields. Some of the popular job opportunities include:

1. Customer Service Executive

The customer service executive is responsible for providing assistance to passengers, handling their queries, and resolving their issues. They must possess excellent communication skills and a pleasing personality.

2. Security Personnel

Security personnel are responsible for ensuring the safety and security of the airport and its passengers. They must have a keen eye for detail and be able to handle stressful situations.

3. Baggage Handler

Baggage handlers are responsible for loading and unloading baggage on the aircraft. They must be physically fit and able to lift heavy loads.

4. Ground Staff

Ground staff are responsible for various tasks such as check-in, boarding, and handling of cargo. They must possess excellent communication skills and be able to work in a team.

5. Airline Ticketing Executive

Airline ticketing executives are responsible for booking tickets, handling cancellations, and providing information to passengers. They must possess excellent communication skills and be proficient in using computer software.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Ranchi Airport offers several job opportunities for 12th pass candidates. The aviation industry is growing, and there is a constant demand for skilled professionals. Candidates who meet the requirements and possess the necessary skills can apply for various job opportunities in Ranchi Airport.
