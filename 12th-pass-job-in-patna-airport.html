12Th Pass Job Opportunities In Patna Airport

Introduction

Patna Airport, also known as Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport, is the main airport serving the city of Patna, Bihar. It is one of the busiest airports in India, serving both domestic and international flights. With the increasing number of travelers, the airport has become a hub for job opportunities. In this article, we will discuss various job opportunities available for 12th pass candidates in Patna Airport.

Cabin Crew

One of the most sought-after jobs in the aviation industry is that of cabin crew. As a cabin crew member, your job will be to ensure the safety and comfort of the passengers on board. To apply for this job, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills. The airline companies conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Ground Staff

Ground staff is responsible for the smooth functioning of the airport. They handle various tasks such as checking-in passengers, baggage handling, security checks, and boarding announcements. To apply for this job, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills. The airport authorities conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Airport Retail

Airport retail is another job opportunity available for 12th pass candidates. As an airport retail employee, your job will be to assist customers in their shopping needs, handle cash transactions, and maintain stock levels. You need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills to apply for this job. Various retail outlets at the airport conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Security Personnel

The safety and security of the passengers and airport premises are of utmost importance. As a security personnel, your job will be to ensure the safety and security of the airport premises by conducting regular checks and monitoring CCTV cameras. To apply for this job, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills. The airport authorities conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Housekeeping Staff

The cleanliness and hygiene of the airport premises are essential for the comfort of the passengers. As a housekeeping staff member, your job will be to maintain the cleanliness of the airport premises by cleaning and sanitizing the toilets, waiting areas, and other public areas. To apply for this job, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills. The airport authorities conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Baggage Handlers

Baggage handlers are responsible for the efficient handling of the passengers' baggage. As a baggage handler, your job will be to load and unload the baggage from the aircraft and transport it to the baggage claim area. To apply for this job, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills. The airline companies conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Electricians and Mechanics

The airport premises require regular maintenance and repair work. As an electrician or mechanic, your job will be to maintain and repair the electrical and mechanical equipment used at the airport. To apply for this job, you need to have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills. The airport authorities and maintenance companies conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for this job.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Patna airport offers a plethora of job opportunities for 12th pass candidates. The aviation industry is expanding, and with it, the job opportunities. The airport authorities and airline companies conduct interviews regularly to select candidates for various job roles. If you have completed your 12th standard and possess good communication skills, you can explore these job opportunities and secure a bright future for yourself.
