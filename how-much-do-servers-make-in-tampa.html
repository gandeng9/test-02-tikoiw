How Much Do Servers Make In Tampa?

Introduction

If you are considering a career as a server in Tampa, you might be wondering how much money you can expect to make. In this article, we will discuss the average salary for servers in the Tampa area and what factors can affect your earnings.

The Average Salary for Servers in Tampa

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the average hourly wage for servers in Tampa is $10.90. However, this can vary depending on the type of establishment you work in and the level of experience you have.

Type of Establishment

Servers who work in fine dining restaurants or upscale establishments may earn more than those who work in fast food or casual dining restaurants. This is because these types of restaurants typically have higher menu prices and customers who are willing to pay more for a quality dining experience.

Level of Experience

Servers who have been in the industry for a longer period of time and have gained more experience may also earn more money. This is because they have developed better customer service skills and have a better understanding of how to upsell menu items.

Tips for Increasing Your Earnings as a Server

If you want to increase your earnings as a server in Tampa, there are a few things you can do:

1. Provide Excellent Customer Service

Customers are more likely to leave a larger tip if they receive excellent service. Make sure to greet customers with a smile, be attentive to their needs, and go above and beyond to make their dining experience enjoyable.

2. Upsell Menu Items

If you can recommend menu items that customers are likely to enjoy, they may be more likely to order them and leave a larger tip.

3. Work in Upscale Establishments

As mentioned earlier, servers who work in fine dining restaurants or upscale establishments may earn more money due to higher menu prices and a more upscale clientele.

4. Work Busy Shifts

Servers who work during busy shifts, such as weekend evenings or holidays, may earn more money due to the higher volume of customers.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the average hourly wage for servers in Tampa is $10.90, but this can vary depending on the type of establishment you work in and the level of experience you have. To increase your earnings as a server, provide excellent customer service, upsell menu items, work in upscale establishments, and work during busy shifts.
