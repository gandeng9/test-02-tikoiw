Introduction

2023 has brought a lot of changes in the job market around Hattiesburg, MS. With the economy picking up, many companies are expanding, and new businesses are emerging, making it a great time for job seekers in the area. In this article, we will provide you with some valuable tips and insights on job hiring around Hattiesburg, MS, to help you find the right job opportunity in 2023.

Job Market Overview

Hattiesburg, MS, has a diverse job market, with opportunities in various industries. The healthcare sector is one of the largest employers in the area, followed by education, retail, and manufacturing. The city also has a growing technology sector, which is attracting many young professionals.

Job Hunting Tips

1. Be Prepared

Before you start your job search, make sure you have an updated resume and cover letter. Research the companies you are interested in and tailor your application to their specific requirements.

2. Use Online Job Boards

There are many online job boards that list job openings in Hattiesburg, MS. Some of the popular ones include Indeed, Glassdoor, and LinkedIn. Make sure you set up job alerts to receive notifications when new jobs are posted.

3. Network

Networking is an essential part of job hunting. Attend job fairs and career events, and connect with people in your industry on LinkedIn. You can also reach out to recruiters and hiring managers directly to inquire about job openings.

4. Be Flexible

Be open to job opportunities in different industries or positions. You might find a job that is a perfect fit for you, even if it is not in your preferred field.

Top Companies Hiring in 2023

Here are some of the top companies that are hiring in Hattiesburg, MS, in 2023:

1. Forrest General Hospital

Forrest General Hospital is the largest healthcare provider in the area, with over 3,500 employees. The hospital is hiring for various positions, including nurses, medical assistants, and administrative staff.

2. University of Southern Mississippi

The University of Southern Mississippi is a public research university that employs over 2,500 people. The university is hiring for faculty and staff positions in various departments, including education, business, and healthcare.

3. Kohl's

Kohl's is a popular retail chain that is expanding its operations in Hattiesburg, MS. The company is hiring for sales associates, cashiers, and management positions in its new store.

4. GE Aviation

GE Aviation is a leading aerospace manufacturer that has a facility in Hattiesburg, MS. The company is hiring for engineers, technicians, and production workers.

Conclusion

2023 is a great year to find a job in Hattiesburg, MS, with many opportunities available in various industries. Use the tips and insights provided in this article to help you find the right job for you. Remember to be prepared, use online job boards, network, and be flexible in your job search. Good luck!
