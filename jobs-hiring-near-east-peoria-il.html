The Job Market in East Peoria IL

East Peoria is a small town in Illinois that has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. With a population of just over 23,000 people, the town is home to several large employers, including Caterpillar Inc. and OSF Saint Francis Medical Center. These companies, along with others in the area, offer a variety of job opportunities for residents of East Peoria and the surrounding communities.

Top Employers in East Peoria IL

Caterpillar Inc.

Caterpillar Inc. is the largest employer in East Peoria, with over 13,000 employees in the area. The company is a global leader in the manufacturing of construction and mining equipment, diesel and natural gas engines, industrial gas turbines, and diesel-electric locomotives. Caterpillar is known for its commitment to innovation and sustainability, and offers a variety of career opportunities in engineering, manufacturing, finance, and more.

OSF Saint Francis Medical Center

OSF Saint Francis Medical Center is a not-for-profit, 616-bed medical center that provides comprehensive healthcare services to the residents of Central Illinois. The medical center is the second-largest employer in East Peoria, with over 6,500 employees in the area. OSF Saint Francis is known for its commitment to patient care and offers a variety of career opportunities in healthcare, including nursing, clinical support, and administrative roles.

UnityPoint Health - Methodist | Proctor

UnityPoint Health - Methodist | Proctor is a network of hospitals and clinics that provides healthcare services to the residents of Central Illinois. The network includes UnityPoint Health - Methodist in Peoria and UnityPoint Health - Proctor in Peoria.
