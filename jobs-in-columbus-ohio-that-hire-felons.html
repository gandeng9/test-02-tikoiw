Jobs In Columbus Ohio That Hire Felons

Introduction

Finding a job as a felon can be challenging, but it is not impossible. Columbus, Ohio, has a variety of industries and companies that are willing to hire individuals with a criminal record. In this article, we will explore some of the jobs available to felons in Columbus, Ohio.

Manufacturing Jobs

Manufacturing companies often hire felons, especially those with experience in the field. Columbus has a strong manufacturing industry, with companies such as Honda, Whirlpool, and Cardinal Health operating in the area. These companies offer a variety of positions, from assembly line workers to skilled machinists.

Construction Jobs

Construction companies are also willing to hire felons, particularly those with experience in the industry. Columbus has a growing construction industry, with companies such as Turner Construction and Danis Building Construction operating in the area. These companies offer a variety of positions, from general laborers to skilled tradespeople.

Food Service Jobs

Restaurants and other food service businesses are another option for felons. Columbus has a vibrant food scene, with many restaurants and bars operating in the area. These businesses offer a variety of positions, from cooks and servers to bartenders and hostesses.

Retail Jobs

Retail companies are also willing to hire felons, especially those with experience in sales or customer service. Columbus has a variety of retail businesses, from small boutiques to large department stores. These companies offer a variety of positions, from sales associates to managers.

Transportation Jobs

Transportation companies, such as trucking companies and delivery services, are often willing to hire felons. Columbus has a strong transportation industry, with companies such as FedEx, UPS, and Schneider National operating in the area. These companies offer a variety of positions, from drivers to dispatchers.

Healthcare Jobs

Healthcare companies are also willing to hire felons, particularly those with experience in the industry. Columbus has a strong healthcare industry, with companies such as OhioHealth and Mount Carmel Health System operating in the area. These companies offer a variety of positions, from nurses and medical assistants to administrative staff.

Conclusion

While finding a job as a felon can be challenging, there are many industries and companies in Columbus, Ohio, that are willing to hire individuals with a criminal record. From manufacturing and construction to food service and healthcare, there are a variety of jobs available to felons in Columbus. By focusing on industries that are more likely to hire felons, and by highlighting any relevant skills or experience, individuals with a criminal record can find meaningful employment and move forward with their lives.
