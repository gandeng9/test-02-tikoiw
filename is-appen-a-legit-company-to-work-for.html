Is Appen A Legit Company To Work For?

Introduction

With the rise of remote work, many people are turning to online platforms to find employment opportunities. One of the most popular platforms for remote work is Appen, a company that hires freelancers for various tasks, including data annotation, transcription, and search engine evaluation. However, with so many online scams and fake job offerings, it's natural to wonder whether Appen is a legitimate company to work for. In this article, we will explore the answer to this question.

The Company Overview

Appen is a global technology and services company that provides data annotation, machine learning, and AI services to businesses and organizations worldwide. The company was founded in 1996 and has since grown to become a trusted name in the industry. Appen has over 1 million freelancers working on its platform and has worked with major tech companies such as Facebook, Microsoft, and Google.

Appen's Reputation

When it comes to online work, reputation is everything. Appen has a good reputation among freelancers, with many positive reviews on websites such as Glassdoor and Indeed. The company has a rating of 3.9 out of 5 on Glassdoor, with many reviewers praising the company's flexible work arrangements and good pay. However, there are also some negative reviews from freelancers who have had issues with payment and communication.

The Hiring Process

Appen's hiring process is straightforward and transparent. Freelancers can apply for open positions on the company's website and will be asked to complete a series of assessments to determine their skills and suitability for the job. Once hired, freelancers will be given access to Appen's platform and can start working on tasks immediately.

Payment and Benefits

One of the biggest concerns when it comes to online work is payment. Appen pays its freelancers on a monthly basis via PayPal or direct deposit, and the payment is always on time. Freelancers are paid based on the number of tasks completed, and the pay rate varies depending on the task and the freelancer's location. In addition to good pay, Appen also offers its freelancers benefits such as health insurance and retirement savings plans.

Training and Support

Appen provides its freelancers with comprehensive training and support to ensure that they are equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to complete tasks successfully. The company also has a dedicated support team that is available to help freelancers with any issues or concerns that they may have.

Conclusion

Based on our research, we can say with confidence that Appen is a legitimate company to work for. The company has a good reputation among freelancers, pays on time, and offers its freelancers benefits and support. However, as with any online work platform, it's important to do your research and read reviews before applying for a job. We hope that this article has provided you with the information you need to make an informed decision about whether Appen is the right company for you.
