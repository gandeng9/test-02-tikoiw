Part Time Jobs In Swiggy Bangalore

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Swiggy is one of the most popular food delivery apps in India. With its presence in all the major cities, Swiggy has become the go-to app for foodies. However, Swiggy has also opened up a new avenue for job seekers. The company offers part-time jobs to students, homemakers, and anyone looking for extra income. In this article, we will discuss part-time jobs in Swiggy Bangalore.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a part-time job in Swiggy Bangalore, you need to fulfill the following criteria:

Age Limit

You should be at least 18 years old to apply for a part-time job in Swiggy Bangalore.

Educational Qualification

There is no educational qualification required to apply for a part-time job in Swiggy Bangalore. However, you should have basic reading and writing skills.

Vehicle

To work as a delivery executive, you need to have a two-wheeler or a four-wheeler with a valid driving license.

Types of Part-Time Jobs in Swiggy Bangalore

Swiggy offers two types of part-time jobs in Bangalore:

Delivery Executive

As a delivery executive, your job will be to deliver food orders to customers. You will be assigned a delivery zone and you need to ensure timely and accurate delivery of orders.

Food Preparation Executive

As a food preparation executive, your job will be to prepare food orders in the kitchen. You will be responsible for ensuring that the food is prepared as per the customer's requirements.

Salary and Benefits

Swiggy offers a competitive salary to its part-time employees. The salary varies based on the job role and the number of hours worked. In addition to the salary, Swiggy also offers the following benefits:

Flexible Working Hours

Swiggy offers flexible working hours to its part-time employees. You can choose to work as per your convenience.

Incentives

Swiggy offers incentives to delivery executives for timely and accurate delivery of orders. The incentives are based on the number of orders delivered.

Insurance

Swiggy provides insurance coverage to its part-time employees. This ensures that the employees are covered in case of any accidents or mishaps while on the job.

How to Apply

To apply for a part-time job in Swiggy Bangalore, follow these steps:

Step 1: Visit the Website

Visit the Swiggy website and click on the 'Careers' section.

Step 2: Select the Job Role

Select the job role that you are interested in - delivery executive or food preparation executive.

Step 3: Fill in the Details

Fill in your personal details, educational qualifications, and work experience (if any).

Step 4: Upload Documents

Upload a scanned copy of your driving license (if applying for a delivery executive role) and a passport-sized photograph.

Step 5: Submit the Application

Submit the application and wait for a response from Swiggy.

Conclusion

Working as a part-time employee in Swiggy Bangalore can be a great way to earn extra income. With flexible working hours and a competitive salary, it is a popular choice for students and homemakers. Follow the steps mentioned above to apply for a part-time job in Swiggy Bangalore.
