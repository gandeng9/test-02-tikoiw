Introduction

If you are a fan of Steve Jobs, you must have watched the 2015 movie that depicts his life history. The movie is an excellent way of learning about the life of a tech genius who changed the world with his inventions. However, understanding the movie can be challenging, especially if you are a student who has to write an essay about it. This article is a comprehensive guide to the Steve Jobs 2015 movie worksheet answers.

The Plot

The movie follows the life of Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Inc. It begins with the launch of the Macintosh in 1984 and ends with the introduction of the iPod in 2001. The movie portrays Steve as a complex character who is brilliant, ruthless, and sometimes cruel to his colleagues and family members.

The Characters

The movie features several characters, including Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, Joanna Hoffman, John Sculley, and Chrisann Brennan. Steve Jobs is the main character, and the movie revolves around his life. Steve Wozniak is his friend and co-founder of Apple Inc. Joanna Hoffman is his marketing executive, John Sculley is his CEO, and Chrisann Brennan is the mother of his daughter, Lisa.

The Themes

The movie explores several themes, including ambition, power, perfectionism, and family. Steve Jobs is portrayed as a man who is obsessed with perfectionism and power. He is willing to do whatever it takes to achieve his goals, even if it means hurting others. The movie also shows the importance of family and how Steve struggles to balance his personal and professional life.

The Quotes

The movie is full of memorable quotes that capture Steve Jobs' philosophy and attitude towards life. Some of the most famous quotes include:

  • "I'm the only one who knows what the future holds."
  • "It's not about money. It's about the people you have, how you're led, and how much you get it."
  • "You can't connect the dots looking forward; you can only connect them looking backwards."

The Lessons

The Steve Jobs 2015 movie is an excellent way of learning about leadership, innovation, and entrepreneurship. The movie shows how Steve Jobs changed the world with his inventions and how he was able to inspire and motivate his team to achieve greatness. It also highlights the importance of perseverance, hard work, and creativity in achieving success.

The Questions

If you are a student who has to write an essay about the Steve Jobs 2015 movie, you may be required to answer several questions. Some of the common questions include:

  • What was Steve Jobs' role in the founding of Apple Inc.?
  • What were some of the challenges that Steve Jobs faced in his career?
  • What were some of the key themes in the movie?
  • What lessons can we learn from Steve Jobs' life?

The Answers

If you are looking for the Steve Jobs 2015 movie worksheet answers, you can find them online. There are several websites that provide comprehensive guides to the movie, including character analysis, plot summary, and discussion questions. Some of the websites that you can check out include IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, and SparkNotes.

The Conclusion

The Steve Jobs 2015 movie is an excellent way of learning about the life of a tech genius who changed the world with his inventions. The movie is full of valuable lessons that can inspire and motivate students to achieve greatness.
